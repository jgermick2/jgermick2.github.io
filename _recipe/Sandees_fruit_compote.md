## Ingredients

16 oz bag frozen sweetened, sliced strawberries, with juice 
2 Fugi or Gala apples, peeled & chunked
2 D'Anjou Pears, peeled & chunked 
1 sm can mandarin oranges, drained 
lots of bananas (5-6), to taste

## Directions

Thaw strawberries in fridge over night. Combine the rest in the a.m. and serve with Belgian waffles and Cool Whip.
