## Background 
	All of these chilles are very mild and not spicy

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup toasted sesame seeds
- 1/2 cup toasted almonds
- 3 cups peanuts
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/4 tsp anise or white pepper
- 3 chiles mulatos (dried)
- 2 chiles pasilla (dried)
- 1 chiles ancho (dried)
- 1 chiles chipotle (dried)
- 1/2 refried plantain
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
- 1/2 onion 
- chocolate to taste
- Chicken stock from boiled chicken 

## Directions

1. Fry all ingredients except raisins, peanuts, cinnamon and chocolate
2. Blend evrything with chicken stock to thicken 
3. Serve with chicken and rice
