## Ingredients

- 2 large cans Mandarin Oranges, drained for 6 hours 
- 16 oz. Cottage Cheese
- 3 oz. box Orange Jello 
- 8 oz. Cool Whip

## Directions

Mix together cottage cheese & box of jello. Add drained oranges.
Fold in the cool whip.

This is best if made the night before.
