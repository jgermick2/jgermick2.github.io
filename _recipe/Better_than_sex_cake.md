## Ingredients

1 German Chocolate Cake
1- 12 oz jar Caramel ice cream topping
1- 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
1-8oz tub cool whip - thawed 
1/2 bag English Toffee bits

## Directions

1. Bake cake according to package directions in 9x13 pan. 
2. While still warm, poke holes with the handle of a wooden spoon. 
3. Pour milk and Caramel sauce over cake and into holes. 
4. When cake is cool, top with whipped topping and sprinkle with toffee bits.
