## Ingredients

- 1 egg (optional)
- 1 cup sugar
- 20 oz. can crushed pineapple 
- 1 cup Vodka
- 2-liter bottle Swiss Creme soda 
- 1 cup Real Lemon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Osterize together the following: egg, sugar, pineapple, lemon juice and vodka. 
2. Pour into punch bowl and add soda carefully (it loves to bubble over).
