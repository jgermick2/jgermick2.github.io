## Ingredients

2 1/4 to 2 1/2 c. flour 
1/3 c. water
2 eggs
1 egg yolk
1 tbsp olive oil 
1 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Combine 1 c. flour and all remaining ingredients. With mixer at low speed, beat 2 minutes. With wooded spoon, stir in additional flour to make a soft dough. 
2. Knead until smooth and elastic.  
3. Cover and let rise 30 min.
