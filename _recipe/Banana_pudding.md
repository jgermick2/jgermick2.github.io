## Ingredients

- 14 oz low-fat sweetened condensed milk
- 1 ½ c cold water
- 3 oz (fat free) vanilla pudding mix
- 2 c Cool Whip (free), thawed 
- 36 (reduced fat) vanilla wafers 
- 3 medium bananas, sliced

## Directions

1. Combine condensed milk, water, and pudding mix in a bowl and mix until smooth. Fold in Cool Whip. 
2. Spoon one cup mixture onto bottom of a 2- quart casserole dish. Top with one-third each of wafers, bananas, and remaining pudding mixture. 
3. Repeat layers two times. 
4. Chill several hours before serving. Refrigerate leftovers.
