## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup soy sauce 
- 1/4 cup lemon juice 
- 1 tbsp chili sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar 
- 1 clove garlic
- 6 loin pork chops

## Directions

1. Combine first 5 ingredients. 
2. Place chops in Ziploc and pour marinade over. 
3. Refrigerate a few hours or overnight.

4. Grill until done.
