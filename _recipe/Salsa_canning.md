## Ingredients

- 15 c. tomatos
- 2 green peppers
- 1 huge sweet onion 
- 4 garlic cloves
- 1 lrg banana pepper 
- 1 lrg cucumber
- 1 red pepper
- 1 yellow pepper
- 2 c. vinegar
- 1/2 c. lemon juice 
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 3 tbsp oregano 
- 1/4 c. salt
- 1/2 bunch cilantro
- 3 - 12oz cans tomato paste

## Directions

1. Chop all ingredients. Combine all except cilantro and tomato paste. 
2. Cook 1 1/2 hours over low heat. 
3. Add Cilantro and tomato paste. 
4. Spoon into pint jars and seal in hot water bath for 15 min.
