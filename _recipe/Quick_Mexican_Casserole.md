## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups (or more) crushed tortilla chips
- 1 large can chicken
- 15 oz. can kidney beans, undrained 
- 15 oz. can black beans, undrained
- 15 oz. can corn with peppers, drained
- 1 cup salsa
- 1 cup chopped onion 
- 1 can green chilies
- 1 Tbl. garlic salt & pepper
- 12 oz. grated cheddar cheese, divided

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a large sprayed pan or roaster with about 1/2 inches of crushed tortilla chips.

2. Mix together all other ingredients except 1/2 of the cheese. Pour onto tortilla chips. Top with remaining cheese. Bake @ 350 for 30 minutes.

NOTE: this was geared towards a son away at school who could slam together an easy meal straight out of the cupboard. Alternatively, one can use any kind of fresh meat and veggies instead.
