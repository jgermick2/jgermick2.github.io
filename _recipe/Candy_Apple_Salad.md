## Ingredients

- 1 large can Pineapple Chunks (drained, but save juice)
- 2 cups Mini Marshmallows 
- 1 Tbl. Flour
- 1/2 cup Sugar
- 1 1/2 Tbl. White Vinegar 
- 1 Egg
- 2 cups Apples, peeled & cut bite- sized
- 1 1/2 cups Cocktail Peanuts 
- 8 oz. Cool Whip

## Directions

1. Mix together pineapple chunks & marshmallows till moist and refrigerate overnight.
2. Cook in sauce pan: pineapple juice, flour, sugar, vinegar, & egg till thick and refrigerate overnight.
3. The next day, combine both mixtures together and add apples, peanuts & cool whip.
