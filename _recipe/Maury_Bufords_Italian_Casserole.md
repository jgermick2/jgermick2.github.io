## Ingredients

- 15 oz. can tomatoes with green chilies
- 1 lb. ground beef, turkey, or venison 
- 2-3 large green peppers, chopped
- 1 medium onion, chopped 
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 can cream of tomato soup 
- 15 oz. can whole kernel corn, drained
- 16 oz. pkg. broad egg noodles, cooked & drained
- 1 tsp. oregano
- 2 cups grated cheddar cheese Black Pepper & Salt

## Directions

1. Break up beef in dutch oven. 
2. Add pepper, onion, garlic, and saute all till beef is browned and vegetables are tender.
3. Pour off any fat. Add soups, corn, & noodles. 
4. Mix gently but well & season with oregano, salt & pepper to taste and mix again. 
5. Top with grated cheese & bake for 2 hours @ 250.
