## Ingredients

- 1 stick butter
- 1 c sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 c sour cream
- 1 tsp baking soda 
- 2 c sifted flour
- 1 tsp baking powder 
- 1 tsp vanilla
- Pinch of salt

Topping:
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 Tbsp butter

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar. Beat eggs well and add to creamed mixture with salt. 
2. Mix baking soda with sour cream. Sift baking powder with flour and add these two mixtures alternately. 
3. Mix lightly, add vanilla. Mix Topping with fork and sprinkle on top before baking. 
4. Bake in 9x9 inch pan at 350⁰ for 40 min.
