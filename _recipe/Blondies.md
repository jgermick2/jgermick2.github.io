## Ingredients

- 2 c brown sugar 
- 2/3 c butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 2 c flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp baking soda Semisweet chocolate chips 
- Chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13x9x2 inch pan, set aside. In a medium saucepan, heat brown sugar and butter over medium heat until butter melts and the mixture is smooth, stirring constantly. 
2. Cool slightly. Stir in eggs one at a time. Stir in vanilla. Stir in flour, baking powder, and baking soda. Spread batter in prepared baking pan. 
3. Sprinkle with chocolate chips and nuts. Bake in a 350⁰ oven for 25-30 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
