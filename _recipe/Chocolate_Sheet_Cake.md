## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cup margarine 
- 1/2 cup cocoa
- 1 cup water
- 2 cup flour (unsifted)
- 1 1/2 cup brown sugar 
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 tsp. cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 can Eagle Brand milk 
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 1 cup confectioners sugar 
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. In small pan, melt 1 cup margarine, stir in 1/4 cup cocoa. Slowly bring to a boil & remove from heat.

2. In large bowl, combine flour, packed brown sugar, baking soda, cinnamon & salt. Add cocoa mixture and beat well. Stir in 1/3 cup of sweetened condensed milk, vanilla, and eggs. Pour into a well-greased 10" X 15" pan and bake @ 350 for 15 minutes.

3. In small pan, melt remaining 1/4 cup margarine & stir in remaining 1/4 cup cocoa and Eagle Brand. Stir in confectioners sugar & nuts. Spread on warm cake.
