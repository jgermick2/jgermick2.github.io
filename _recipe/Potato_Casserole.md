## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (32 oz)frozen hash brown potatoes
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup onion
- 2 cups sour cream
- 1 can cream of chicken soup 
- 1 stick of butter (melted)
- 2 cups grated cheddar cheese 
- Crushed corn flakes
- 1/4 cup butter

## Directions

1. Put potatoes in large bowl (slightly defrosted), add onions and salt. 
2. Mix soup with sour cream in small bowl (add a little milk). 
3. Add sour cream, cheddar, and melted butter to potatoes. 
4. Salt and pepper to taste. 
5. Pour into casserole dish. Mix 2 cups of crushed corn flakes with half a stick of melted butter.
6. Sprinkle on top. Bake in 350 oven for 1-1 1/2 hours.
