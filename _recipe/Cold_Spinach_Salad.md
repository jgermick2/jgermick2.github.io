## Ingredients

10 oz. bag of Spinach 
1 cup Salads Oil
1/4 cup Sugar 
1/3 cup Catsup 
1/4 cup Vinegar
1 tb. Worcestershire Sauce
1 medium onion, finely chopped Salt, to taste
6 strips Bacon, crisp & cut up 
2 hard-boiled Eggs, chopped

## Directions

1. Wash & drain spinach thoroughly. Tear into pieces into salad bowl & set aside. 
2. Now combine together oil, sugar, catsup, vinegar, worcestershire sauce, and onion and refrigerate until ready to serve. 
3. At serving time, pour on dressing. Sprinkle bacon & chopped eggs over salad.
