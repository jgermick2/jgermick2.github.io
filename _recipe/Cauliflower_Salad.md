## Ingredients

- 1 Lrg cauliflower head, chopped fine 
- 1 lrg green pepper, chopped
- 1 sm onion, diced
- 1 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled 
- 1 cup shredded Colby cheese

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Combine 1 cup mayonnaise with 1/4 cup sugar. Spoon over top. 
3. Refrigerate 4 hrs.
4. Toss before serving.
