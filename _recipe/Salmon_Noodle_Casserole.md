## Ingredients

- 1 can (15 oz) salmon
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/2 tsp dry mustard
- 1 tall can (13 oz) pet evaporated milk
- 1 package (10 oz) mixed vegetables, thawed & drained
- 3 cups wide or medium noodles cooked
- 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

## Directions

1. Drain salmon; break into large chunks. Melt butter in saucepan; stir in flour and dry mustard. Gradually stir in evaporated milk. 
2. Cook and stir over medium heat until slightly thickened. Stir in vegatables, cooked noodles and 1 cup cheese. 
3. Gently fold in salmon. Pour into greased 2 quart baking dish. Bake covered, at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. 
4. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup of cheese. Bake uncovered 5 minutes or until cheese melts.
