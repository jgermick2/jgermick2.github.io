## Ingredients

- 2 - 1 lb. Pound Cakes or Angel Food Cakes 
- 2 containers Sure Gel
- 2 qt. fresh strawberries 
- 16 oz. Cool Whip

## Directions

1. Cut the cakes into 1" squares.
2. Cut the strawberries into bite-sized pieces and mix with the Sure Gel.
3. Layer half of the cake into a pretty glass bowl, followed by half the strawberry mixture and then half the Cool Whip. Repeat. 
4. Refrigerate.
