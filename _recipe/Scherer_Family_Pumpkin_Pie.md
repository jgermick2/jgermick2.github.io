## Ingredients

- 1 lrg can pumpkin 
- 4 eggs, well beaten 
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar 
- 1/2 tsp salt, heaping
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 1/2 to 2 tbs pie spices 
- 2 to 3 Tbs flour
- 2 1/2 cups scalded milk, cooled. Uncooked pie shell

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients. 
2. Pour into pie shell. 
3. Bake 425 degrees for 15 min, then 350 for 45 min.
