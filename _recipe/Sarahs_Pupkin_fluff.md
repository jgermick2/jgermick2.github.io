## Ingredients

- 14 oz can Pumpkin puree
- 1 sm pkg instant Vanilla Pudding 
- 8 oz Cool Whip
- 1/2 tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice 
- 1/2 tsp Cinnamon

## Directions

Mix all together and serve with sliced apples, graham cracker bears, and Nilla Wafers. The little guys love this!
