## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup granulated sugar 
- 1/2 tsp. vanilla
- 2 eggs, unbeaten
- 1-1/4 cups sifted flour
- 1 tsp. soda 
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. cinnamon 
- 3 cups Quick Oats 
- 1/2 cup nuts

## Directions

1. Cream shortening, sugars, vanilla and eggs.
2. Sift flour, soda, salt, cinnamon. Add to creamed mixture. Fold in uncooked oats and nuts. 
3. Drop on greased cookie sheet. Flatten if desired. Bake 350 deg. 12-15 minutes. Cool 2 minutes on pan.
