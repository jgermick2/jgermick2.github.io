## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (3 oz) cream cheese, softened 
- 1/4 c. real bacon pieces
- 2 tablespoons choped onion
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 1 pkg (8 oz) refrigerated crescent rolls

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350. 
2. Combine cream cheese, bacon, onion, and pepper. 
3. Pinch crescent rolls into two rectangles. 
4. Spread cheese mixture on each rectangle.
5. Roll jelly roll fashion and pinch edge to seal. 
6. Cut into 16 slices. Place on baking pan. 
7. Bake for 15 minutes.
