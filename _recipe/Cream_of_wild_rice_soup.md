## Background

I picked up this recipe while living in Minnesota. For a meatier soup, I sometimes add cooked chicken as well.

## Ingredients

- 3 c. cooked wild rice (1 c. rice:3-4 c. water)
- 1 large onion, finely diced 
- 1 carrot, finely diced
- 1 rib celery, finely diced
- 1 c. ham, diced 
- 1/2 c. butter
- 1 c. flour
- 8 c. chicken broth
- 1 c. cream or half and half

## Directions

1. In 4-5 qt. kettle, saute the onion, carrot, celery, and ham in butter 3 min, or until tender. 
2. Sift in flour, stirring and cooking until the flour is blended but not brown. 
3. Slowly add broth, stirring until blended. Add wild rice, season as desired. Heat thoroughly. 
4. Add cream and reheat gently but do not boil.
