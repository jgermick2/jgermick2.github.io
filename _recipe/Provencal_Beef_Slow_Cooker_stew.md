## Ingredients

- 1 lb lean beef round, cut into 1-inch cubes (London Broil)
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
- 2 medium garlic cloves, minced
- 2 large carrots, sliced (about 1 cup) 
- 12 oz canned pinto beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 1/2 cup canned beef broth
- 14 1/2 oz canned crushed tomatoes (about 1 1/2 cups)
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano, crushed 
- 1/4 tsp dried thyme, crushed 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. Place beef in a 5-quart slow cooker. 
2. Coat a 12-inch non-stick skillet with cooking spray. Add onion, mushrooms, and garlic. Saute over medium-high heat 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
3. Add to slow cooker. Add carrots. Place half of beans in slow cooker. Place remaining beans in blender. 
4. Add 1/2 cup of broth to blender and puree. Add mixture to slow cooker. Add remaining broth, tomatoes, oregano, dried thyme, salt and pepper. 
5. Cover and cook on high setting of slow cooker for 6-7 hours. Yields about 2 cups per serving.
