## Ingredients

- 4 (8oz) tubs whipped cream cheese 
- 1 pint sour cream
- ¼ lb butter
- 1 ½ c sugar
- 2 Tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp lemon juice 
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Into a large mixing bowl, empty the tubs of cream cheese and the container of sour cream. Do not mix. Place butter and sugar in bowl then sift cornstarch into the bowl and sprinkle on the vanilla and lemon juice. Allow all ingredients to stand unmixed until they reach room temperature.
2. With an electric mixer, beat all ingredients together until mixed well. There will still be some lumps.
3. Beat eggs into mixture one at a time, mixing well after each addition After all the eggs have been added, the mixture should be perfectly smooth.
4. Pour into a buttered 10-inch spring form pan. Place the pan into a larger roasting pan and half fill with hot water.
5. Bake in a 375 degree oven for one hour. Remove from the oven. Let cool in the water bath for 20 minutes, then remove and cool to room temperature in the spring form. 
6. After cooling, refrigerate at least three hours before serving, but remove the cake from the refrigerator at least 20 minutes before serving to take off the chill. The cake may be garnished with fruit as desired.
7. You may alter the flavoring by using ¼ tsp almond extract instead of vanilla or 3 tbsp dark rum or flavored liqueur instead of the extract. 
8. The cake has no crust, but you may sprinkle the buttered spring form pan with ground or finely chopped nuts, or fine dry breadcrumbs or cake crumbs. (I put it in a graham cracker crust)
