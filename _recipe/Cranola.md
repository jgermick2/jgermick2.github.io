## Ingredients

½ cup canola oil 
1/3 cup honey
¼ tsp salt
¾ tsp cinnamon 
1/3 cup skim milk
¾ cup brown sugar
5 cups whole oats ( enough so dough is not soupy )
½ cup each of any of the following: sliced almonds, chopped walnuts, pecans, sunflower nuts, raisins, craisins, dates, dried fruit, Wheat germ, flaxseed

## Directions

1. Mix first 6 ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat until 
2. sugar melts and everything is blended. 
3. Remove from heat. Add oats and mix well. 
4. Spread over 2 cookie sheets sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. 
5. Bake in a 375 degree oven until brown, 10-15 min. Let cool. Add other ingredients. 
6. Store in covered container.
