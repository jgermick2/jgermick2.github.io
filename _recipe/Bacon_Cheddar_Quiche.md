## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. Bacon, fried and chopped 
- 1/2 lb. shredded Cheddar Cheese 
- 1/4 cup chopped Onions
- 2 1/2 cups Milk
- 1 1/2 cups Bisquick 
- 5 large Eggs
- 1/5 tsp. each, Salt & Pepper

## Directions

1. Sprinkle bacon, cheese & onions into bottom of a greased 9" X 13" pan. Beat remaining ingredients until smooth. 
2. Pour into pan. Bake @ 350 for 35 - 40 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
