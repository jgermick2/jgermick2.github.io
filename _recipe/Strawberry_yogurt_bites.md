## Ingredients

3 ( 8oz ) containers strawberry yogurt
1 pkg ( 3 oz ) JELL-O Strawberry flavor gelatin
8 fresh strawberries

## Directions

Mix yogurt and dry gelatin in microwaveable bowl. Microwave on HIGH 5 min or until gelatin is completely dissolved, stirring every 2 min. Spoon into 16 paper lined mini muffin cups. 
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm. Remove from liners, top with strawberries.
