## Ingredients

- 4 Cornish hens 
- salt and pepper
- 2 c. seasoned croutons 
- 1/2 c. wheat germ
- 1/2 c. finely chopped celery 
- 1/2 c. chicken broth
- 6 tbs butter
- 1 tsp sugar

Sauce:
- 1 10oz jar red currant jelly, 
- 1/2 c. golden raisins, 
- 1/4 c. butter, 
- 2 tsp. lemon juice, 
- 1/4 tsp allspice.

## Directions

1. Sprinkle cavity of each hen with salt and pepper. Combine wheat germ, celery, croutons, broth, 2 tbsp butter, sugar, 1 tsp salt, and 1/8 tsp pepper. Spoon into body cavities. Tie legs together. Brush with melted butter.
2. Roast, breast side up, in open roasting pan at 425 for 1 hour. Combine all sauce ingredients. Cook, stirring occasionally, about 10 min. over medium low heat. During last 15 min of roasting, cut strings and brush with sauce. Serve with sauce.
