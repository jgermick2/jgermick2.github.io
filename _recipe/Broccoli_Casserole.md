## Ingredients

- Fresh or frozen broccoli cooked about 10 min and drained well
- Velveeta Cheese 
- 1 stick of butter
- 1 roll of Ritz Crackers that has been crushed

## Directions

1. Place cooked and drained broccoli in greased (or sprayed) casserole dish. 
2. Slice the cheese and cover all the broccoli. 
3. In a non-stick skillet, melt the butter and brown crushed crackers for about 5-10 minutes. 
4. Then place crackers on top of cheese and bake at 350 deg for about 30 minutes.
