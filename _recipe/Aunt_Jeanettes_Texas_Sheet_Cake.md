## Ingredients

- 2 c. flour
- 2 c. sugar
- 1 stick butter 
- 1/2 c. shortening 
- 4 tbs cocoa
- 1 c. cold water 
- 1/2 c. buttermilk 
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp cinnimon 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda 
- 2 eggs

Frosting:
- 1 stick butter
- 4 tsp cocoa
- 5 tbs milk
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 lb powdered sugar 
- 1 c. chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Sift flour. Add 2 c. sugar. 
2. In saucepan, melt 1 stick butter, shortening, cocoa, and water. Mix well. Heat to melt. Add buttermilk, vanilla, cinnimon, salt, soda, and eggs. 
3. Mix into dry ingredients.
4. Pour in well greased 15 x 10 x 1 in pan. Bake 30 min @ 400. Combine and boil frosting butter, cocoa, and milk. 
5. Remove from heat. Add vanilla, powdered sugar, and nuts. Spread over hot cake.
