## Ingredients

- 2 c flour
- 2 tsp baking soda 
- 2 tsps cinnamon 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3 eggs 
- 3/4 c oil
- 3/4 c buttermilk 
- 2 c sugar
- 2 tsps vanilla
- 8 oz crushed pineapple, drain 
- 2 c raw carrots, chopped
- 3 1/2 oz shredded coconut 
- 1 c walnuts, chopped

Buttermilk Glaze: 
- 1 c sugar
- 1/2 tsp baking Soda 
- 1/2 c buttermilk 
- 1/2 c butter
- 1 tbsp corn syrup 
- 1 tsp vanilla

Cream Cheese Frosting: 
- 1/2 C butter
- 8 oz cream cheese 
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 c powdered sugar 
- 1 tsp orange juice 
- 1 tsp orange peel

## Directions

1. Grease 9x13 pan. Sift flour, soda, cinnamon, and salt. Set aside. Beat eggs. Add oil, buttermilk, sugar, and vanilla. Add flour mixture, pineapple, carrots, coconut, and walnuts. Pour into pan. Bake at 350° for 55 min. 
2. While cake is baking, prepare buttermilk glaze. In small saucepan, combine sugar, soda, buttermilk, butter, and corn syrup. Bring to a boil. Cook 5 min, stirring occasionally. 
3. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Remove cake from oven and slowly pour glaze over hot cake. Cool cake until glaze is totally absorbed. Prepare cream cheese frosting. Cream butter and cream cheese until fluffy.
4. Add remaining ingredients and mix until smooth. Frost cake in pan. Refrigerate until set. Serve chilled.
