## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk 
- 2 Tbl. Hershey's syrup
- 1 1/2 cups whipping cream 
- 1/2 tsp. coconut extract
- 1 cup whiskey

## Directions

1. Beat eggs. Add remaining ingredients and beat all. Refrigerate & serve.
