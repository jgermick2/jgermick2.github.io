## Background

Mom gave me this recipe. I believe this is the apple crisp recipe that would turn Mom's tongue blue.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sour cream 
- 1 tsp baking soda 
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 2 cups chopped apple 
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- dash nutmeg & cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix sugar & cream. Stir in soda & salt. Stir apples into flour and fold into sugar mixture. 
2. Spread evenly in a greased 8" square pan or baking dish. Sprinkle with nutmeg & cinnamon.
3. Bake in 350 degree oven for 25 minutes. Serve warm with cream.
