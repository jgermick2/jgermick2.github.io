## Ingredients

- 1 lb fresh pork sausage 
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 can tomato sauce
- 1 can sliced mushrooms, drained 
- 2 tsp oregano
- 1/8 tsp garlic salt
- 1/2 c. grated parmesan
- 2 c. finely shredded mozzarella cheese
- 9 English muffins, split and toasted

## Directions

1. In large skillet, cook pork sausage for 10 min. 
2. Add ground beef and cook until brown. Drain. 
3. Combine tomato sauce, mushrooms, oregano, garlic salt, and meat. 
4. Place muffins on baking sheet and spread with 1/3 c. meat mixture. 
5. Sprinkle with parmesan and top with mozzarella. Bake 5-8 min. in 400 degree oven.
