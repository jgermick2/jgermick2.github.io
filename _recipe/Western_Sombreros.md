## Background

Another "Family Favorite". Originally from Mom, it has become a "go to" recipe for many of us.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 c. celery 
- 1/4 c. onion
- 1/4 c. green pepper 
- 2 tbs butter
- 1 lb ground bee
- 1 tsp chili powder
- dash hot pepper sauce 
- 1 lb can tomatos
- 1 (8oz) can tomato sauce corn chips
- shredded lettuce
- shredded cheddar cheese

## Directions

1. Cook celery, onion, and green pepper in butter. 
2. Add meat. Cook until meat is browned. 
3. Add seasoning, and tomatos and sauce. Cook 45 min - 1hr.
4. Serve over corn chips. Top with lettuce and cheese.
