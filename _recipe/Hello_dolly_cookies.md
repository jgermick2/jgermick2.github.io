## Ingredients

- 1 stick margarine
- 1 cup crushed graham crackers 
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 cup coconut
- 6 oz. chocolate chips
- 6 oz. butterscotch chips
- 1 can sweetened condensed milk

## Directions

1. Melt margarine in bottom of 9 X 13 pan. 
2. Spread in separate layers, each of the next 5 ingredients. 
3. Pour milk over top. 
4. Bake 25 minutes @ 350.
