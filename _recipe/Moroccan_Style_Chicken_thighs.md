## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground cardamon 
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 
- 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper 
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 8 chicken thighs, skinned
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1(15oz) can chickpeas, drained 
- 1(14.5 oz c) can diced tomatoes, drained
- 2 cups sliced zucchini 
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Combine first 6 ingredients in a small bowl; rub chicken with cumin mixture. 
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over meduim-high heat. Add chicken, and saute 3 minutes on each side. 
3. Add chickpeas and tomatoes; cook 5 minutes. Add zucchini and broth; bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 10 minutes over low heat. Stir in lemon juice.
4. Serve with couscous.
