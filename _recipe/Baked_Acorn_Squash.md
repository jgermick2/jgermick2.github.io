## Background

This is Mom's Recipe

## Ingredients

- Acorn Squash 
- brown sugar 
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash squash. Cut in half. Remove seeds. 
2. Sprinkle brown sugar on squash, add pat of butter.
3. Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees) about 1 1/2 hours
