## Ingredients

Filling:
- 6 c. apples 
- 1/2 c. sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon

Topping: 
- 1/3 c. butter 
- 1/2 c. sugar 
- 3/4 c. flour

UnBaked pie crust

## Directions

1. Combine filling ingredients. 
2. Place in unBaked pie crust. 
3. Cut butter into sugar and flour. 
4. Cover filling with topping. Bake at 400 for 40-50 min.
