## Ingredients
- 1 lb. ground beef
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs 
- 1/3 cup minced onion 
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 Tbl. parsley
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1/8 tsp. pepper
- 1/2 tsp worcestershire 
- 2 Tbl. shortening
- 12 0z. bottle chili sauce 10 oz. jar grape jelly

## Directions

1. Mix everything but jelly & chili sauces together. 
2. Roll into small meatballs (makes about 5 dozen) and brown them in shortening. 
3. Put jelly & chili sauce in a pan and stir until melted. 
4. Add meatballs and simmer 30 minutes.

TIP: Rolled out meatballs CAN be Baked on a cookie sheet for about 20 minutes @ 350, if desired, and simmered in the melted sauce in a crock pot.
