## Ingredients

2 cups sugar
1 cup butter
3 eggs
1 cup water
3 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 tsp cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves

## Directions

Bake at 350 in two medium sized pans.
