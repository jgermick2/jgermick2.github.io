## Ingredients

- 2 cans whole green beans
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cubed american cheese 
- 1 can french fried onion rings

## Directions

1. Drain beans, reserving 1/2 cup liquid. 
2. Place beans in casserole. 
3. Sprinkle 1/2 can onion rings over beans. 
4. Then sprinkle with cheese. 
5. Pour diluted soup over all and bake at 350 til cheese melts. Sprinkle on onions and cook 5-10 min. more.
