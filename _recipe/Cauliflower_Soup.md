## Ingredients

- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 1 large head cauliflower (about 2 1/2 lbs), cored and chopped
- 5 cups chicken broth

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a soup pot over medium heat.
2. Add the onion and cauliflower and cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is softened but not browned, about 10 minutes. 
3. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil. 
4. Reduce the heat and simmer until the cauliflower is softened, about 20 minutes.
5. Puree the soup in batches in a blender until smooth. Season to taste with salt.
