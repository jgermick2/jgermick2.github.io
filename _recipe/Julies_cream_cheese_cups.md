## Ingredients

- 1 pkg Dunkan hines moist deluxe, yellow, or devils food cake mix.
- 1/4 c. margarine, melted
- 2-8 oz cream cheese (softened)
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 c. sugar 
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 1/2 c. sour cream 
- Cherry pie filling

## Directions

1. Combine cake mix and margarine. Mix at low speed 1 min. Spoon into mini muffin cups
2. Combine remaining ingredients except sour cream and pie filling. Mix until smooth. Divide into muffin cups. 
3. Bake for 20 min. at 350 until set.
4. Top with sour cream and cherry pie filling. Keep refrigerated.
