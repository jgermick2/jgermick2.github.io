## Ingredients

- 16 oz baby carrots 
- 4 tbsp butter
- 2 1/2 tbsp honey
- tbsp lemon juice
- 1/16 tsp ground ginger

## Directions

1. Combine carrots and enough water to cover in a large saucepan. Cook over medium-high heat until tender, about 5 minutes.  Drain well.
2. Combine butter and honey in a large skillet. Cook over low heat, stirring continually, until well blended.
3. Add lemon juice and ginger to butter mixture and mix well. Add the carrots to the skillet and stir to glaze. Cook stirring occasionally until heated through.
