## Ingredients

- 2 c. heavy cream
- 1/4 c. powdered sugar 
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tbsp raspberry liqueur 
- 2 c. raspberries, washed
- 25 thin chocolate wafers, such as Nabisco Famous Chocolate Wafers.

## Directions

1. Beat cream, sugar, vanilla, and liqueur for several minutes until stiff peaks form. Transfer 2/3 of whipped cream to a small bowl and set aside.
2. On low speed, add 3/4 c. of the raspberries to the cream remaining in the bowl. Mix just to crush them slightly. Set aside.
3. Spread 1 tbsp of raspberry cream on a chocolate wafer. Repeat to form a vertical stack of 6 cookies and cream. Arrange the stack horizontally on a platter. Continue adding wafers and cream to make a log as long as the platter. Leaving the last cookie plain, press it against the raspberry filling. 
4. Spread the reserved whipped cream over the top, sides, and ends of the log. Scatter the remaining raspberries over the log. 
5. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours or until cookies soften slightly. Cut diagonally into slices and serve.
