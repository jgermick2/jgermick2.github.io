## Ingredients

- 12 oz can frozen orange juice concentrate
- 12 oz can frozen lemon juice
- 46 oz can pineapple juice 
- 6 c. water
- 1 c. sugar
- 1 bottle ginger ale

## Directions

1. Mix and freeze all ingredients except gingerale. Pour Gingerale over frozen mixture.
