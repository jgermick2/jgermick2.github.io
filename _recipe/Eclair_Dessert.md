## Ingredients

- 1 lb box graham crackers
- 2 small packages instant French vanilla pudding
- 3 ½ cups milk
- 1 (8oz) Cool Whip

Frosting:
- 2 pkgs baking chocolate 
- 2 tsp white Karo syrup 
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 3 tbsp softened margarine 
- 1 ½ cups powdered sugar 
- 3 tbsp milk

## Directions

1. Butter bottom of 9 X13 pan. Line with graham crackers. Mix pudding with milk. Blend with cool whip.
2. Pour ½ over crackers. Place another layer of crackers. Pour over remaining pudding. Cover with layer of crackers. 
3. Refrigerate for 2 hours. 
4. Beat Frosting ingredients until smooth. Spread on cake. 
5. Refrigerate 24 hours.
