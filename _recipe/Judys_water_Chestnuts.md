## Ingredients

- 2 cans whole water chestnuts 
- 1 lb. bacon, cut into thirds
- 1/3 cup each: mayo, brown sugar, and chili sauce toothpicks

## Directions

1. Wrap whole chestnuts in bacon, secure with toothpick
2. bake @ 400 for 15-20 minutes till bacon is about done. (This can be done a day ahead, if desired)

3. Mix together mayo, brown sugar, and chili sauce.
4. Pour over water chestnuts and bake @ 350 for 15 min. before serving.
