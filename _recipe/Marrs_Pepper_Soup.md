## Background
This is a family favorite. Then and now!

## Ingredients

- 2# ground beef
- 2 tbs chopped onion 
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk 
- 1/2 cup rice
- 3/4 cup bread crumbs
- salt/pepper
- 8-10 large green peppers 
- 1 large can tomatoes
- 1 onion sliced 
- 1/2 cup rice

## Directions

1. Combine ground beef, chopped onion. eggs, bread crumbs, 1/2 cup rice, milk, salt & pepper.
2. Fill peppers with meat mixture. Put in enough boiling salted water to cover peppers. 
3.  Boil for 15 minutes. Skim. 
4. Add sliced onion & tomatoes. 
5. Bring to boil and simmer for 1-1/2 hours. Add 1/2 cup rice and cook for additional 1/2 hour. 
6. Add more rice if thicker soup desired. Form any remaining meat mixture in balls and cook in soup.
