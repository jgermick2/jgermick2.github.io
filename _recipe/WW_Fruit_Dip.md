## Ingredients

- 2 c. fat free Greek Yogurt, plain
- 3/4 c. water
- 1 sm box sugar free, White Chocolate instant pudding

## Directions

1. Mix all and refrigerate. 
2. Serve with grapes, strawberries, apples, pineapple, kiwi or any other fruit of your choice.
