## Ingredients

- 1 c. Brown sugar 
- 1/2 c. Butter
- 1 tsp vanilla 
- 2 1/2 c flour 
- 1/2 tsp salt 
- 4 tbs cocoa 
- 1/2 c sugar
- 1/2 c oil 
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 c buttermilk 
-  1/2 tsp cinnamon 
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 2-2 1/2 c grated zucchini Chocolate chips

## Directions

1. Cream together br sugar, sugar, butter, and oil. Add eggs, vanilla, buttermilk. Mix well. Sift in dry ingredients. Add zucchini and mix. 
2. Pour batter into 9x13 pan. 
3. Sprinkle with chocolate chips. 
4. Bake 45 min at 325°.
