## Background

This soup was a good Friday supper when I grew up. I still get hungry for it and make it for myself.

## Ingredients

1 large potato 
1/4 cup raw rice
1 small onion diced 
1 can tomato soup

## Directions

1. Cook potato, rice & onion in salted water until potato is done.
2. Brown l generous tbs. of butter in frying pan with heaping tsp of flour. 
3. Add l can of tomato soup. Stir.
4. Thin with potato water and add to potato/rice mixture.
