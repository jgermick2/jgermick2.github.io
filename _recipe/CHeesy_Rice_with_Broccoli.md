## Ingredients

1 (10-oz) pkg frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
3/4 cup chopped processed cheese 
3 tbsp butter, divided
1/4 chopped onion
1 1/2 cups hot, cooked instant rice 
1 (8 oz) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1/3 cup whipping cream 
1 tsp lemon pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375 deg. 
2. Grease a 1 1/2 quart baking dish. 
3. Microwave broccoli following package directions; drain. 
4. Add cheese and 2 tbsp butter to broccoli. 
5. Microwave on HIGH, stirring frequently, until cheese is melted, about 2 minutes.

6. Meanwhile melt remaining butter in a small skillet over medium-high heat. 
7. Saute onion in butter, stirring continually, until tender, about 2 minutes.

8. Combine broccoli mixture, onion mixture, rice, water chesnuts, whipping cream and lemon pepper in a large bowl and mix well. 
9. Spoon into prepared baking dish. Bake for 15 minutes.
