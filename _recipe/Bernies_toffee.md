## Background

A friend of Mom's, Bernie Nelson, brought this recipe into our family and I think of her every year when I make it. It has become a required Christmas treat around my house.

## Ingredients

- 2 sticks butter
- 1 c. sugar
- 4 oz melted milk chocolate
- 1/4 c. water 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 c. nuts, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients except chocolate and nuts in a heavy saucepan. 
2. Boil, stirring constantly, to hard crack (300 degrees). 
3. Pour immediately onto ungreased 9x13 pan. 
4. Cool slighly. Top with melted chocolate and nuts.
5. When cool, crack into bite sized pieces.
