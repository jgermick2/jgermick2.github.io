## Background

This is Maureen Mulholland's recipe.

## Ingredients

Vegetable mixture 
- 2 cups cut broccoli
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme, 
- crushed 1 small bay leaf
- dash garlic powder
- 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
- 1/2 cup chopped onion

Roux mixture:
- 2 tbsp butter or margarine 
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- dashes white pepper 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. In a sauce pan combine chicken broth, chopped onion, and vegetable mixture. Bring mixture to boiling. 
2. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until vegetable is tender - then remove bay leaf.

3. Place half of the vegetable mixture in a blender. 
4. Cover and blend for 30 - 60 seconds till smooth. Pour into a bowl. Repeat the rest if desired - or keep some chuncky.

5. In saucepan, melt butter. 
6. Blend in flour, salt and pepper. Add milk all at once. 
7. Cook and stir until mixture is thickened & bubbly. Stir in blended vegetable mixture. 
8. Cook & stir until soup is heated through. Season to taste with additional salt & pepper if desired.

Note: Can substitute desired vegetable.
