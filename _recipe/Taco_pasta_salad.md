## Ingredients

- 1 lb Tricolor Spiral Noodles, cooked 
- 1 lb Ground Beef
- 1 pkg Taco Seasoning Western/Catalina Dressing Chopped Tomatoes
- 1/2 c. chopped onion Shredded Cheddar Chopped Green Peppers Black Beans, rinsed Black Olives

## Directions

1. Cook ground beef with taco seasoning, as directed on package.
2. Then mix all together (except for cheese) while meat & noodles are still a little warm. 
3. Cool before adding the cheese so it won't melt.
