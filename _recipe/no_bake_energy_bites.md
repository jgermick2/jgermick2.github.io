## Ingredients

- 1 cup oatmeal
- 1 cup toasted coconut flakes (optional)
- ½ cup chocolate chips
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup ground flaxseed 
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1 tsp vanilla nuts (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir all ingredients together. 
2. Let chill in refrigerator for half an hour. 
3. Roll into balls. 
4. Store in airtight container and keep refrigerated for up to one week.
