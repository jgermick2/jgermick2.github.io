## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter 
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp baking powder 
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream together butter and sugar. Beat in egg until fluffy. 
2. Sift together salt, baking powder and flour into mixture. Add vanilla. 
3. Divide in half and shape into logs. 
4. Wrap in plastic and freeze. Slice 1/4" or use a spritzer. Decorate with sugar or sprinkles. 
5. Bake at 350 deg for 8-10 minutes.
