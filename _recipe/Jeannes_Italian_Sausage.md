## Background

This fantastic recipe is great for a crowd. Robert even made it in a dutch oven at camp!

## Ingredients

- 5 lbs Italian sausage 
- 1 lrg can pear tomato
- 1 (6oz) can tomato paste 
- 1 lrg can tomato sauce 
- 2 lrg onions, chopped
- 3 stalks celery
- 3 green peppers, chopped 
- 1 c. water
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder 
- 1/2 tbsp fresh parsley 
- 1/2 tsp oregano
- 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning

## Directions

1. Prick sausage. 
2. Bake at 300, covered, for 45 min-1 hour. 
3. Do not add water. 
4. Remove and cut into 2 in pieces. 
5. Drain grease and return sausage to pan.
6. Combine all ingredients. 
7. Pour over sausage and bake, covered, at 300 for 1 1/2 hours more.
