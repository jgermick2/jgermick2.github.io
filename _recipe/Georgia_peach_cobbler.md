## Background

Beth Germick learned this recipe while living in Covington, Georgia. Rumored to be the recipe from the "Blue Willow Inn" (a local "Southern Cooking" favorite), you can't beat it for an easy, tasty treat. Tip: You can vary the amount of peach juice you use to make it more or less soupy.

## Ingredients

- 28 oz sliced peaches, undrained (reserve 2 tbs.)
- 1 c. sugar
- 1 c. self-rising flour
- 1/2 c. melted butter (divided)

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Mix sugar, flour, and 1/4 c. butter. Put 1/3 of mixture on bottom of 9x13 pan. Add undrained peaches. Top with remaining flour mixture. Srinkle with remainder of butter and sugar. Bake 30-40 minutes. 
3. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!
