## Ingredients

1 pkg Jiffy white cake mix
21 oz can blueberry pie filling 
1/2 c. Butter, melted
1/2 c. pecan pieces

## Directions

1. Pour pie filling into an un-greased 8" square pan. Dump box of cake mix over top. Sprinkle nuts over cake mix and pour melted butter over all. 
2. Bake @ 350 for 45 - 50 minutes. I like to serve it with cool whip or ice cream.

Just double the recipe if you want to fill a 9 X 13 pan. Also, I have used Cherry pie filling or Yellow cake mix and it always tastes great!
