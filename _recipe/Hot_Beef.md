## Background

I'm submitting this one but I can't take any credit for it. I got it from Mom and I think almost all of us make it. Served on some nice hearty rolls, it is great for a crowd.

## Ingredients

- 4 lbs boneless rump roast 
- 3 c. hot water
- 3 beef bullion cubes 
- 1 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1 1/2 tsp oregano dash garlic powder
- 3 lrg green peppers cut in thin slices

## Directions

1. Brown meat at 450 for 30 minutes in uncovered roaster. 
2. Combine water and next 5 ingredients. 
3. Pour over meat, turn oven down to 350, and cook, covered, until meat is tender (3 hours). 
4. In the last 1/2 hour, add peppers.
