## Ingredients

- 6 tbsp butter, softened 
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar 
- 1 large egg
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 cup plus 
- 1 tbsp unbleached flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda 
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon 
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 6 oz butterscotch chips

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375. Combine butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar in a large bowl. Beat until smooth. Add egg and vanilla; beat well.
2. Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt in a medium bowl.
3. Gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture and stir until blended. Stir in oats and butterscotch chips.
4. Drop by tbsp onto an ungreased baking sheet. Press lightly with fingers. Bake until the edges begin to brown, about 9 minutes.
