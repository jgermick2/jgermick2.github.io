## Background 
    6lbs of pork spare ribs is what this is generally for, I like 1 tsp of salt per pound of ribs, for people who like less salt you could probably do half or a quarter this amount.

## Ingredients

- 6 lbs of pork spare ribs
- 3 Tbsp. dark brown sugar ( or 2 Tbsp of granulated with 1-2 Tbsp of Molasses) 
- 1 Tbsp of Chinese five spice ( plus 1 or 2 tsp extra based on taste)
- 1 reserve tsp of Chinese five spice
- 1/3 or 1/2 cup Hoisin sauce ( alternatively Oyster sauce, but might only want 1/4 to 1/5 a cup of this)
- 1/3 or 1/2 cup of soy sauce
- 3 Tbsp. honey
- ginger ( close to same volume of garlic, or to taste)
- 3-7 cloves of garlic ( to taste)
- sesame seeds (optional)
- green onions (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix (1 Tbsp of five spice, sugar, and salt ), rub all over meat. 
2. place rips on wire rack on a baking sheet, add 1 cup of water.
3. Seal tightly with foil
4. bake at 250-300 for 2-4 hours depending on oven and size of meat (if you have more time lower temperature longer time is best)
5. When meat is to your liking take out of oven.
6. mix remaining ingredients in sauce pan and simmer to homogenized , add extra water, soy sauce, or honey depending on your mood.
7. cover meat with glaze, put meat back in oven without foil under broiler or simply high heat for 3-5 mins.
8. Watch it! don't let it burn!
9. When ready to serve top with sesame seeds and green onions.
