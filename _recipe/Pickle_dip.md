## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup finely chopped dill pickles
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon dill weed
- ¼ cup dill pickle juice, or to taste

## Directions

1. Mix sour cream, cream cheese, pickles, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, and dill weed together in a bowl. 
2. Stir dill pickle juice into the dip in small increments to taste. 
3. Chill to allow flavors to blend.
