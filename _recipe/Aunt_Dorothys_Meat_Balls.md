## Ingredients

- 2 lbs hamburger 
- 2 pkg onion soup 
- 1 can evap. milk
- 1 tbs. worcestershire sauce 
- 8 drops tabasco
- 2 eggs

sauce:
1 c. brown sugar 2 c. ketchup

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients except sauce. 
2. Roll into 1 in balls. 
3. Bake on foil lined cookie sheet at 350 for 1/2 hour or until cooked through. 
4. Combine sauce ingredients and pour over meatballs. 
5. Simmer 20 minutes.
