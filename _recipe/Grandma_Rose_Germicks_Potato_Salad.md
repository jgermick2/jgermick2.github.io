## Ingredients

- 5 lb bag red potatoes, peeled and boiled in salt water
- 1 bunch celery, chopped
- 32 oz Kraft Mayonnaise
- 2 dozen hard boiled eggs, chopped

## Directions

Mix all ingredients. Refrigerate.
