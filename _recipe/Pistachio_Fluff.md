## Ingredients

- 20 oz can Crushed Pineapple
- 3 oz box pistachio pudding mix 
- 8 oz Cool Whip
- 1/2 pkg Mini Marshmallows (10.5 oz size) 
- 1 cup chopped Walnuts, if desired

## Directions

Mix together in order listed. Chill and enjoy!
