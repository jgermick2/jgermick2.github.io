## Ingredients

Cream together:
- ½ cup soft butter
- ½ cup oil
- 1 ½ cups sugar

Add:
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ cup sour milk or buttermilk

Sift:
- 2 ½ cups flour 
- 4 tbsp cocoa
- 1 tsp baking soda
- ½ cinnamon
- ½ tsp cloves
- ½ tsp salt

- 2 c shredded zucchini
- ½ cup chopped nuts 
- 1 cup chocolate chips

## Directions

1. Add to creamed mixture. 
2. Stir in 2 cups shredded zucchini. 
3. Put in greased and floured 9 X 13 pan. 
4. Sprinkle on ½ cup chopped nuts and 1 cup chocolate chips. 
5. Bake 45 min at 325 degrees.
