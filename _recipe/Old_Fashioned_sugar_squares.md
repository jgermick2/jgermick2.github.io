## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. butter or margarine, softened
- 1 1/4 cups sugar 
- 1 egg
- 2 cups flour
- 1/4 tsp baking soda 
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 5 tsp milk
- 1 Tbs vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream butter with 1 cup of sugar. Add egg and beat until light. Sift dry ingredients and add alternately with milk. 
2. Spread in 15x10x1 in baking pan. Sprinkle remaining sugar on top.
3. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 min. Cut into squares.
