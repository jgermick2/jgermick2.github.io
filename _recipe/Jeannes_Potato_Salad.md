## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boiled, cooled, and cubed potatos
- 1/4 c. celery 
- 1/4 c. onion
- 1 green pepper
- 2 tbs pimento, chopped
- 3 hard boiled eggs
- 2 chopped sweet pickles 
- 2 tbs sweet pickle juice 
- 1 c. mayonnaise
- 2 tbs. vinegar

## Directions

Combine all ingredients. Chill.
