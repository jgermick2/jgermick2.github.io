## Ingredients

- 1 small head of cabbage or 1 pkg shredded cabbage
- 4 green onions - chopped 
- 1 c. slivered almonds
- 8 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1 pkg of dry Ramen chicken flavored noodles
- 2 cooked chicken breasts

Dressing:
- 1 c. oil
- 4 tbsp seasoned rice vinegar 
- 1/4 c. sugar
- 1 tsp black pepper
- the package of seasoning from the Ramen noodle soup

## Directions

1. Toast almonds and sesame seeds until lightly browned in 350 degree oven about 8 minutes. 
2. Toss and chill salad. Add dressing, almonds, and seeds and serve.
