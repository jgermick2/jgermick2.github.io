## Ingredients

- 1 can cream of chjcken soup 
- 8 oz. sour cream
- 2 lbs chicken breasts, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces 
- 1 sleeve Ritz Crackers
- 1 stick of melted margarine 
- 1 tbsp poppy seeds

## Directions

1. Blend soup and sour cream. Mix in chicken. 
2. Place in 9x13 pan. 
3. Crush crackers and mix with poppy seeds and melted margarine. Sprinkle over chicken. 
4. Bake at 350 for 30 min. Great to make ahead and refrigerate.
