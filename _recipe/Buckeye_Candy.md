## Ingredients

- 2 cups creamy peanut butter
- 1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened 
- 3 3/4 cups powdered sugar
- 2 cups (12 oz pkg) semi-sweet chocolate morsels 
- 2 tbsp vegetable shortening

## Directions

1. Beat peanut butter and butter in large bowl until creamy. Beat in powdered sugar until mixture holds together and is moistened. Shape into 1-inch balls; place on prepared baking sheets. Freeze for one hour.

2. Melt morsels and shortening in medium, microwave-safe bowl on HIGH for one minute; stir. Morsels may retain some of their shape. If necessary, microwave at additional 10 to 15 sec intervals, stirring until melted.

3. Dip peanut butter centers into melted chocolate using a wooden pick, leaving a portion of the center uncovered. Shake off excess chocolate and scrape bottom of candy on side of bowl. Return to baking sheets; refrigerate until chocolate is set. Store in covered container in refrigerator.
