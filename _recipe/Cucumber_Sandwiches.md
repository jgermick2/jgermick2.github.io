## Ingredients

- 8 oz. cream cheese
- l pkg. Italian Dressing Mix (oil & vinegar) 
- dill weed
- cucumbers 
- party rye bread

## Directions

1. Mix 8 oz. cream cheese with l package of Italian Dressing (oil & vinegar.) 
2. Refrigerate. 
3. Spread on party rye. Add slice of cucumber. Sprinkle with dill weed.

Whole package of dressing mix may be too salty. Add to taste.
