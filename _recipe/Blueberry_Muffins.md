## Background

Kay Germick's recipe.

## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/4 cup vegtable oil 
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup blueberries

## Directions

1. Beat egg with fork. Stir in milk & oil. Blend flour, sugar, baking powder & salt. Add to liquid & stir just enough to moisten dry ingredients. Add berries. 
2. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
