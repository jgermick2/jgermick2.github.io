## Ingredients

cup brown sugar 
-1 cup oil
-2 eggs
-2 cup oats
-1 ½ cup flour
-1 tsp baking soda
-1 cup raisins or dates 
-1 cup chopped nuts
-1 ½ tsp ground cloves and cinnamon
-¼ tsp salt

Honey Glaze:
-¼ cups honey 
-2 tbsp butter

## Directions

1. Heat oven to 350⁰. Grease jelly roll pan. Mix brown sugar, oil eggs in large bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients. Spread in pan with spatula.
2. Bake 17-22 minutes. Cool. Drizzle with honey glaze.

HONEY GLAZE
1. Heat in pan over medium heat stirring constantly until butter is melted and mixture is heated through.
