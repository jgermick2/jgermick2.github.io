## Ingredients

- 18 oz cream cheese, room temperature 
- 3 sticks of butter, room temperature
- 3 cups all-purpose flour 
- 2 cans Solo Filling powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix cream cheese and butter until creamy. Add flour one cup at a time. Blend well. 
2. Flour surface and rolling pin and roll dough cut into 2-3 inch squares.
3. Put 1/2 tsp filling into center of square and pinch opposite corner of dough in the center over filling. 
4. Bake 350 degrees for 15 minutes. When cool sift powdered sugar on top.
