## Background

This one is one of my personal favorites. It is out of this world when made with the homemade pasta dough but it is still good and easier to use boxed shells or manicotti noodles. Chop everything in a food processor and it can be done in no time.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp chopped green onion 
- 1 10oz pkg chopped spinach, drained
- 1 c. finely chopped cooked chicken 
- 1/2 c. finely chopped cooked ham 1/2 c. grated Parmesan
- 1 beaten egg
- 1 tbsp dry sherry
- 1/4 tsp ground ginger salt
- salad oil

Parmesan-Cheese sauce: 
- 1/4 c. butter
- 1/4 c. flour
- 1 1/2 c. half and half 
- 1 1/2 c. water
- 2 chicken bouillon cubes 
- 1/2 c. grated Parmesan

Homemade pasta dough
5 qts water

## Directions

1. Cut prepared dough into 3 pieces. Roll 1 piece into 16x8 rectangle. Cut into eight 4 in squares. Repeat with remaining dough. 
2. Cover, let stand 30 min. In saucepan, cook onion in butter until tender. Stir in next 7 ingredients and salt to taste. 
3. Preheat oven to 350. Grease 13x9 pan. In 8 qt saucepot, heat water, 2 tbsp salt, and oil to boiling. 
4. Cook a few pasta squares at a time, 5 min. Remove with spoon and drain. Assemble while warm. 
5. Spread tablespoon of meat mixture across center of warm pasta. Roll. Place seam side down in pan. Repeat. Prepare sauce and pour over cannelloni.
6. Sprinkle with Parsley. Bake 20 min, then broil 5 min.

Sauce: 
1.  In saucepan, stir flour into hot butter. Gradually stir in half and half, water, and boullion. 
2. Cook, stirring constantly, until sauce is thickened. Stir in Parmesan.
