## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs large uncooked shrimp 
- 1/2 cup margarine or butter
- 1 garlic clove, minced 
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1/2 tsp ground red pepper
- 1 tsp ground black pepper 
- 1 tsp thyme
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce 
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Shell and devein shrimp. 
2. In 1 quart saucepan, over low heat, cook garlic in hot butter until lightly browned. Stir in ketchup, remaining ingredients, and salt. Cook mixture over low heat 2 minutes to blend flavors. Stir constantly. 
3. Thread shrimp onto skewers. Brush butter mixture over shrimp. Broil shrimp 5-8 minutes or until opaque, brushing occasionally with butter mixture. Can also be grilled.
