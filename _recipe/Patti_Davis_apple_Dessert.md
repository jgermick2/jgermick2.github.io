## Ingredients

- 1 lb butter
- 1 cup brown sugar 
- 1 cup oats
- 4 cups flour
- 1-1 /2 cups sugar 
- 1-1/2 cups water 
- 3 Tbs corn starch 
- 1 Tsp cinnamon
- approx. 8 apples

## Directions

1. In saucepan, mix sugar, cornstarch, and cinnamon. Add 1 1/2 cups of water. Stir and heat until thick. Mix butter, brown sugar, oats, and flour with hands. 
2. Reserve 2 cups for top. Press remainder in bottom of 9x13 pan.
3. Peel and slice apples. Place on dough. Pour melted sugar mixture over apples and top with remaining crumble mixture. 
4. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
