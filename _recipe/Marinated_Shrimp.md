## Ingredients

- 3 cloves garlic 
- 1/3 cup oil
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce (or ketchup) 
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 2 tbsp fresh basil 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper 
- 2 lbs shrimp

## Directions

Good marinade for shrimp on the grill or sauteed.
