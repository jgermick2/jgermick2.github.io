## Ingredients

- 4 Pork Chops, 
- 1/2" - 3/4" thick Ranch Dressing

Crumb Mixture:
- 1 1/2 cups Bread Crumbs, seasoned panko is good
- 1/2 pkg Good Season Italian Dressing mix
- 1 Tb Parmesan Cheese 
- 1/2 Tb Powdered Garlic

## Directions

1. Dip chops in Ranch dressing and then coat with crumb mixture.
2. Bake @ 400 for 45 minutes on cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
