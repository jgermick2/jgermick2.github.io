## Ingredients

6 lb. ground beef, browned & drained 
2 medium onions, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped 
salt & pepper, to taste 
1/4 cup mustard,
1-2 cups catsup
1/8 to 1/4 cup sugar
2 doz. hamburger buns

## Directions

Add all to browned beef and simmer for about 1 1/2 hours.
