## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (8oz) cream cheese
- 1 jar Kraft ROKA Blue Spread 
- 1/2 tsp garlic
- 3/4 c chopped pecans 
- 1/4 c dried cranberries Crackers

## Directions

1. Mix 1st 3 ingredients.
2. Refrigerate 2 hours. 
3. Shape into ball and coat with remaining ingredients. 
4. Serve with crackers.
