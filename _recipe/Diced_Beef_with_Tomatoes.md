## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 lb beef cubes
- 14 oz canned tomato soup 
- 14 oz canned tomatoes 
- 3/4 cup celery, chopped
- 14 oz can mushrooms 
- 1/2 tsp ginger
- 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce 
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 3 tbsp vinegar garlic salt to taste salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 F. 
2. In a large skillet, heat oil, melt butter and sear beef cubes. Put the seared meat in a casserole dish and keep warm in oven.
3. Degrease skillet, add all other ingredients and bring to a boil. 
4. Pour mixture over beef cubes, then cook in oven for 1 hour. Transfer to a hot serving dish.
