## Ingredients

- 1 lb Turkey, Venison, or Ground Beef
- 1 can Black Beans, rinsed 
- 1 c. chopped onion
- 1 Tbsp. chili powder
- 1 tsp. Kosher Salt 
- 1/2 tsp. Black Pepper 
- 2 cups Tomato Sauce
- 15 oz can diced Tomatoes with green chilies

## Directions

1. Saute meat & onions. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Simmer 20 minutes.
