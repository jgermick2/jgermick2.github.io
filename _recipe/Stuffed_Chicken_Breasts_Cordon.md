## Ingredients

- 4 boned chicken breasts, 
- 6 oz each 4 slices ham
- 3 1/2 oz grated cheddar cheese 
- 3 tbsp flour
- 3 eggs, beaten 
- salt and pepper
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs 
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tsp oil
- cream of mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 325 F. Skin chicken breasts, slit and spreadh butterfly-style. Place a slice of ham in each breast. Sprinkle with grated cheddar, and press firmly to close.

2. Dredge each stuffed breast with flour and dip into beaten eggs. Season with salt and pepper, then coat with breadcrumbs.

3. In an overproof skillet, heat oil, melt butter and gently sear chicken breasts on both sides until golden. Finish cooking in oven approximately 25 minutes.

4. Pour cream of mushroom sauce over top and serve.
