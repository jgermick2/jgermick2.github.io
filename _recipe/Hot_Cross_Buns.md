## Ingredients

- 2 tbs. yeast
- 3/4 c. lukewarm milk 
- 1/4 c. brown sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 6 tbs. butter 
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/3 c. confectioners sugar
- 1 tsp. cinnamon 
- 1/2 tsp nutmet
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves 
- 1/4 tsp. allspice
- 4 - 4 1/2 c. flour 
- 1/2 c. raisins
- 2-3 tsp milk
- egg wash (1 egg + 1 tsp water and salt to taste)

## Directions

1. Proof yeast in milk with a tsp of sugar for 15 or til foamy. Stir in remaining sugar, eggs, butter, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice. Add flour, 1 cup at a time, til dough is soft and elastic. 
2. Turn in bowl and coat with butter. Cover and let rise 1 hour or until doubled. 
3. Punchdown, flatten into a rectangle 1/2 in. thick. Sprinkle with raisins. Roll into 21 in. log and cut crosswise into 16 pieces.
4. Form buns and arrange seam side down on buttered and floured baking sheets. Let rise, covered loosely, until almost double.
5. Brush with egg wash. Cut a cross into top of each and bake in 425 oven for 15 minutes until rolls sound hollow when bottoms are tapped. 
6. Cool on racks. In small bowl, beat confectioners sugar with milk to form a thick icing.
7. Drizzle into the crosses.
