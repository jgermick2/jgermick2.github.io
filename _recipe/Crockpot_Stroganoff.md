## Ingredients

- 2 lb. beef stew meat (or meatballs or chicken)
- 1 can Golden Mushroom soup 
- 1 can Cream of Onion soup
- 8 oz. sliced mushrooms (or canned mushrooms, drained)
- medium onion, diced
- 16 oz. medium-sized egg noodles 
- 8 oz. cream cheese
- 8 oz. sour cream

## Directions

1. Put meat, soup, mushrooms, and onion in slow cooker on low, all day. 
2. Add sour cream and cream cheese 1 hour before serving. 
3. Boil pasta, drain and add to stroganoff. 
4. Let set for about 20 minutes and serve.
