## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs round steak 
- 2 1/4 cup water
- 2 tsp sugar 
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp cornstarch 
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- Quartered tomatoes and pepper strips (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown meat. Add 1 cup water, sugar and salt. Cover. Cook over low heat for 1 hour or until tender. 
2. Combine cornstarch, soy sauce and remaining water. Blend. Add to meat. Cook and stir until thickened. 
3. If desired, add quartered tomatoes and pepper strips. Cover for 5 minutes. 
4. Serve with rice or noodles.
