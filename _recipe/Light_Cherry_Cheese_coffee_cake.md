## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (8 oz) light cream cheese, softened
- 1/3 c. powdered sugar 
- 1 egg, separated
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1 pkg refrigerated french bread dough
- 1 c. light cherry pie filling

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350. Combine cream cheese, sugar, egg yolk, and extract until smooth. 
2. Carefully unroll french bread dough and place over baking stone or cookie sheet. spread cream chees evenly down center of dough; about 4 inches wide. 
3. Top with pie filling. Cut 10 slits, 1 1/2 inches wide on each long side of dough surrounding cherry mixture. 
4. To braid, fold strips of dough at an angle across cherry mixture, alternating dough strips from one side to the other. Fold ends over to seal. 
5. Beat egg white and brush on dough. 
6. Bake 25-30 minutes. Serve warm.
