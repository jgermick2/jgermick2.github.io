## Background

This is a great VERY simple, plain stuffing. You can add just about whatever you want to it, to make it more exciting - sauted onions and celery, chopped apple, etc. You can replace the water with melted butter, too. You can add whatever seasoning you like, too. Poultry seasoning is mostly sage, I think. I like this for it's simplicity.

## Ingredients

- 1 (1 pound) loaf white bread 
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 tsp poultry seasoning
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper 
- 1/4 cup water

## Directions

1. Moisten all the bread with as much water as is needed to make moist. 
2. Add the onion, seasoning, and salt and pepper. Mix with hands. Place in turkey, or in foil and wrap up and cook for at least one hour, longer if you are cooking it in the turkey. 
3. Open the foil for the last 15 minutes to make the top crusty.
