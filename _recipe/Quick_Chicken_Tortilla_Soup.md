## Ingredients

- Bear Creek Tortilla Soup 
- 1 can tomatoes with chilies
- 1 can whole kernel corn, 
- drain 1/2 lb cooked chicken
- small bag tostitos, crushed

## Directions

1. Cook soup as directed. Add next 3 ingredients and simmer 1 hour. 
2. Serve with plenty of crushed tostitos on top.
