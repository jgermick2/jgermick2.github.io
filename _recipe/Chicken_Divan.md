## Ingredients

1 pkg. frozen broccoli spears
2 chicken breast, boned & cooked 
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup mayo OR sour cream
1/2 tsp. lemon juice 1/2 tsp. curry powder
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1/4 cup soft bread crumbs
2 Tbl. melted butter
1/2 tsp. lemon juice 1/2 tsp. curry powder
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1/4 cup soft bread crumbs
2 Tbl. melted butter


Modifications: -> To turn this dish into a "Memorial Day Weekend" - sized casserole, use:

3.5 lb fresh broccoli crowns, lightly steamed
6 lb chicken breasts, cooked & shredded in advance 4 cans cream of whatever soup
16 oz sour cream
2/8 oz cans or 1 lb fresh mushrooms
And I fake the other ingredients proportionately

## Directions

1. Cook broccoli in boiling, salted water. 
2. Drain and arrange in shallow 1 1/2 qt. baking dish. 
3. Simmer chicken in small amount of water for 35 minutes till done. 
4. Drain, cut up & arrange over broccoli. 
5. Mix next 4 ingredients and pour over top. 
6. Sprinkle with cheese & bread crumbs mixed with butter.
7. Bake for 25 minutes @ 350. Serve over rice.
