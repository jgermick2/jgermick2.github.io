## Ingredients

- 1 can green beans 
- 1 can yellow beans 
- 1 can kidney beans 
- Sweet onion 
- Green pepper 
- Celery
- 1/4 cup oil
- 1/2 cup vinegar 
- 1/2 cup sugar

## Directions

Drain beans. Slice sweet onion, green pepper and celery. Add to beans. Mix oil/vinegar/sugar and pour over beans.
