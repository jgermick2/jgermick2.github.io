## Background

This recipe originated in the Chicago Tribune in 1972 and became known as "Aunt Dorothy's Delicious Apple Coffeecake". The same dough without the sugar makes an excellent pizza base.

## Ingredients

- 1 pkg dry yeast
- 2 tbsps each warm water, sugar 
- 1 egg, slighly beaten
- 1/2 c. milk, scalded then cooled 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 c. butter
- 2 c. sifted all purpose flour
- 6-8 cooking apples, pared, cored, and sliced

Topping:
- 3/4 cup sugar 
- 1 tsp cinnamon 
- 1/4 c. butter

## Directions

1. Disolve yeast in water. Let stand for 5 min. Add sugar, egg, milk and salt. Cut in 1/2 c. butter into flour as for pie crust. Add yeast mixture, beat.
2. Spread dough in a buttered jelly roll pan 15x10 1/2 x 1n. Cover and let rise until doubled in bulk (45 min). Top with apples. Mix sugar with cinnamin for topping. Sprinkle over apples. Dot with 1/4 c. butter. 
3. Bake at 375 for 25 min or until brown.
