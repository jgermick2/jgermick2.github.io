## Ingredients

- 3 cups brown sugar
- 3 cups butter or margarine 
- 6 cups quick cook oatmeal
- 1 Tbs baking soda 
- 3 cups flour granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in a bowl and mash, knead, and squeeze.
2. Roll into small balls (midway between walnut and filbert size). Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Rub bottom of a small glass with butter and dip in granulated sugar. Press each ball flat. 
3. Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 min.
