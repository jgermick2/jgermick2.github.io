## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar 
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 stick of butter
- 1 cup chocolate bits 
- 2 cups quick oats
- 1 cup chopped nuts 
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Directions

1. Combine sugars, milk, and butter in saucepan. Bring to a boil. Cook 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. 
2. Add remaining ingredients. Drop by rounded teaspoons onto waxed paper.
