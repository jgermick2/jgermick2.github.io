## Ingredients

- 3 sweet potatoes (1 1/2 lb), peeled and cubed
- 3 tbsp red wine vinegar 
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped 
- 2 tbsp raspberry jam
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard 
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- red or yellow pepper
- chopped bunch of scallions

## Directions

1. Put potatoes in salted water on low boil, cook for 10 minutes, drain. 
2. In small bowl, whisk vinegar, garlic, jam, and mustard together. Whisk in olive oil. 
3. Put potatoes and mixture in bowl and toss to coat.
