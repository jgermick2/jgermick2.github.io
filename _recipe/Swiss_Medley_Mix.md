## Ingredients

- 16 oz. bag frozen Broccoli/Cauliflower/Carrots, thawed
- 1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup 
- 1 cup shredded Swiss Cheese
- 1/3 cup Sour Cream 
- 1/4 tsp. Black Pepper
- 4 oz. drained pimento (optional)
- 2.8 oz. can French Fried Onion Rings

## Directions

1. Combine vegetables, soup, sour cream, pimento, half the cheese, and half the onion rings. 
2. Pour into a 1 qt. casserole, covered, and bake @ 350 for 30 minutes. 
3. Uncover, top with the remaining cheese & onions and continue baking for 5 more minutes.
