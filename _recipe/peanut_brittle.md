## Ingredients

- 2 cups Sugar
- 1 cup White Karo Syrup 
- 1 tsp. Water
- 1 tsp. Baking Soda 
- 1 lb. raw Peanuts
- 3 Tbl. Butter

## Directions

1. Combine sugar, karo, water & mix in heavy skillet. 
2. Boil for 2-3 minutes. Add peanuts & cook till hard. 
3. Remove from heat and quickly stir in butter & soda. 
4. Spread on 2 cookie sheets.
