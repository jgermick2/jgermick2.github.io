## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cooked chicken 
- 4 hard boiled eggs, chopped
- 2 cups diced celery
- 1/2 cup chopped toasted almonds
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp grated onion 
- 2 tbsp lemon juice 
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup crushed potato chips 
- 1/2 grated cheese

## Directions

1. Combine chicken, eggs, celery, almonds, salt, onion and lemon juice in bowl; fold in mayonnaise. 
2. Turn into greased casserole; top with potato chips and cheese. 
3. Bake at 450 deg for about 12 minutes or until salad is bubbly and cheese is melted.
