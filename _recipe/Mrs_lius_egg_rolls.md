## Background

A neighbor from Hong Kong taught Jeanne Marrs to make these authentic egg rolls.

## Ingredients

- 1/4 c. green onion, chopped 
- 1 head celery cabbage
- 8 oz mushrooms, chopped 
- 10 oz shrimp, chopped
- 2 c. bean sprouts 
- 1 lb ground beef
- salt and pepper to taste 
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder 
- 1 tbsp accent
- 1 tbsp sugar
- red pepper to taste
- egg roll wrappers

## Directions

1. Saute onion. Add chopped celery cabbage, mushrooms, shrimp and sprouts. Set aside. 
2. Brown ground beef. Drain. Add to mixture. 
3. Cook, stirring, for 5- 10 min. Add seasonings and stir. 
4. Roll in wrappers. Moisten edges with water and seal rolls. 
5. Fry in hot oil until browned.
