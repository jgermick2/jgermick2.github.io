## Ingredients

- 1 box frozen puff pastry sheets, thawed
- 1 envelope Knorr Vegetable recipe mix
- 1 c mayonnaise 
- 8 oz sour cream
- 8 oz shredded Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Unfold pastry sheets and roll each sheet into 12 X 8 inch rectangle. 
3. Cut each rectangle into 24 squares, about 2 inches each. Press squares into mini muffin pans.
4. Combine remaining ingredients in medium bowl. 
5. Generously fill pastry shells with vegetable mixture. 
6. Bake 15 minutes or until golden brown and puffed.
