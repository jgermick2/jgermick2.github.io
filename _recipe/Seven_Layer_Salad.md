## Ingredients

- 1 med head lettuce - torn in small pieces
- 1/4 to 1/2 chopped green pepper 
- 1/2 cup chopped green onions 
- 1/2 cup chopped celery
- 1 pkg. frozen peas - defrosted - not cooked
- 2 cups mayonnaise (or 1 cup + 1 cup sour cream)
- 2 Tbs sugar 
- 1/2 salt
- 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese 
- 8 slices bacon, fried crisp & crumbled

## Directions

1. Layer first 5 ingredients in order given. 
2. Mix mayonnaise (sour cream) sugar and salt. 
3. Spread on top. 
4. 6th layer - spread on cheddar cheese, 
5. 7th layer top with crumbled bacon.  
6. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
