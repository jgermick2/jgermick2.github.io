## Ingredients

- 2 lbs beef stew meat 
- carrots
- celery 
- onion 
- potatoes 
- peas 
- mushrooms
- salt and pepper 
- 1 tbsp ketchup
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce 
- 1 tsp gravy coloring
- corn starch

## Directions

1. Brown meat. Cover with water. Add carrots, celery, onion, salt and pepper. 
2. Simmer 1 1/2 hours. Add potatoes. Simmer 1/2 hour. 
3. Add peas and mushrooms. Heat. Stir in ketchup, worsh. sauce, and gravy coloring.
4. Thicken with corn starch mixed with cold water, if desired.
