## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (6 oz) broad noodles, cooked and drained.
- 1 c. small curd cottage cheese 
- 1/2 c. evaporated milk
- 3 eggs, well beaten 
- 1/4 c. butter
- 1/2  tsp  salt 
- 1/8 tsp pepper.

## Directions

1. Combine noodles with remaining ingredients. 
2. Turn into greased casserole. Bake at 350 for 1 hour or until golden brown and custard is set.
