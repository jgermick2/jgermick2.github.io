## Background

Mom used to make this soup. She got it from her sorority, Pi Epsilon Kappa.

I never made this soup, but always liked it. It's much different from the normal bean soup. If Mom were around I would ask her about the garlic. It seems 6 to 8 pieces of garlic is too much. You must cut 6 to 8 pieces from a clove of garlic.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups mixed dried beans
- Ham bone, pickled pork or small sausage
- 2 stalks celery
- 2 large onions chopped 
- 1 bell pepper chopped 
- 6 - 8 pieces garlic
- 1 tsp paprika

## Directions

Soak beans overnight in large pot.

1. Drain; add water, ham bone, onion, pepper, celery and garlic. Simmer 2 1/2 hours; stirring and adding water when needed. 
2. Add tomatoes and seasonings. Simmer 1 hour. 
3. Add juice of 1 lemon and chopped parsley JUST BEFORE SERVING.
