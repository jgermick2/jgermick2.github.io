## Background

Harry's Chocolate Shop was a popular hang out at Purdue University and many of us enjoyed these wings. Bill Caldwell "learned" this recipe while he was a student at Purdue.

## Ingredients

- 6 oz soy sauce
- 6 oz Red Hot chili sauce 
- 2 oz honey
- 1/8 tsp garlic salt 
- 1/8 tsp ginger
- 10 lbs chicken wings
- bleu cheese dressing celery sticks

## Directions

1. Bake or fry chicken wings.
2. Combine all ingredients except dressing and celery sticks. 
3. Dip wings into sauce.
4. Serve with celery sticks and bleu cheese dressing
