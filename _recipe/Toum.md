## Ingredients

- 4 cloves garlic minced with 
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 cup canola oil
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1-2 egg whites (as needed)

## Directions

1. In a food processor, add garlic minced with salt, begin processor and slowly add oil and lemon juice, about 2 Tbsp at a time, alternating them until gone. 
2. Scrape down sides. 
3. Process until it reaches mayonnaise consistency. Add one to two egg whites while the processor is blending	(if needed). 
4. Lasts about a week refrigerated.
