## Background

Mom gave me this receipe. She served it to me a couple of times. I liked it. I only made it once as I recall.

## Ingredients

- 1 small head of cabbage, shredded 
- 1 lb of ground beef or chuck
- 1 cup diced onions 
- 1 cup rice
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper 
- 2 cans tomato soup
- 1 can stewed tomatoes 
- 2 cups water
- 1/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage, set aside. Brown meat and onions.
2. Place shredded cabbage in greased 9x13 pan covering entire bottom. 
3. Spoon meat & onions, rice, salt and pepper over cabbage. 
4. In a small saucepan, heat soup, stewed tomatoes and water. 
5. Bring to a boil. Pour over casserole mixture. Sprinkle cheese over entire top. Cover with foil, bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hrs.
