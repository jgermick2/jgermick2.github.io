## Ingredients

- 2 small boxes French vanilla pudding
- 8 oz. Cool Whip
- 1 large can pineapple chunks, with juice
- 1 large can sliced peaches, with juice 
- 1 can mandarin oranges, drained well

## Directions

1. Mix together pudding and cool whip. Then add remaining ingredients. 
2. Refrigerate for a couple hours and serve.
