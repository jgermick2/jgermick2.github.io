## Background

This is Delores Mold's recipe

## Ingredients

- 5 sweet potatoes
- 1 stick butter or margarine 
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 6 marshmallows
- 7 marshmallows

## Directions

1. Bake 5 sweet potatoes in foil, each wrapped separately. 
2. Peel potatoes & slice in casserole dish. 
3. Heat 1 stick of butter or margarine, add 1 1/2 cups brown sugar and 6 marshmallows. 
4. Melt marshmallows in mixture and add to sweet potatoes. Heat till warm in oven. 
5. Then add about 7 marshmallows, cover, heat until marshmallows melt.
