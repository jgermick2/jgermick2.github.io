## Ingredients

- 1 c. washed, uncooked rice 
- 1 sm onion, chopped
- 1/4 lb butter
- 1-4 oz can mushrooms and juice
- 1 can campbell's consomme 
- 1 can cold water
- 1 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Brown rice and onion in butter. Add remaining ingredients. 
2. Bake, uncovered, at 350 for 1 1/2 hours.
