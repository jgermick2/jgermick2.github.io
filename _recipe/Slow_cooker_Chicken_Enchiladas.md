## Ingredients

- 5 chicken breasts (may be still frozen)
- 1 can sliced mushrooms, drained 
- 1 can cream of chicken or mushroom soup
- 1 pkg. Lipton's onion sup mix 
- 2 cans green chilies
- 9 huge or 12-15 small flour tortillas 
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1/2 jar salsa verdi
- 8 oz. shredded Mexican cheese

## Directions

1. Slow Cook first 5 ingredients for 5 - 7 hours. Shred chicken and add sour cream.
2. Spray bottom of 9" X 13" pan and cover bottom with salsa verdi. Fill tortillas and lay in pan. 
3. Top with Mexican cheese and bake, covered with foil, @ 350 for 1/2 hour.
