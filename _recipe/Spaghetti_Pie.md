## Ingredients

- 6 oz thin spaghetti, cooked and drained
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese 
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- 1 lb hamburger
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt 
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp garlic salt 
- 3/4 tsp oregano
- 2 cups whole tomatoes 
- 1 cup cottage cheese
- 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Directions

1. Mix spaghetti, butter, Parmesan cheese and eggs. Spread in 8x12 baking dish to form crust. 
2. Saute hamburger, onion and green pepper in skillet until browned. Drain. Add salt, sugar, garlic salt, oregano and tomatoes to hamburger, mix well. 
3. Spread cottage cheese over spaghetti in dish. Then spread hamburger mixture on next. 
4. Sprinkle cheese over the top. Bake at 350 deg for 30-35 minutes.
