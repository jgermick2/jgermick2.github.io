## Background

These savory muffins are great with your meal in place of rolls!

## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat flour 
- 2/3 cup flour
- 2/3 cup packed brown sugar 
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice 
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 3/4 cup raisins, optional

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 deg. Grease 6 (4 in) muffin cups. 
2. In large bowl, combine flours, sugar baking soda and pumpkin pie spice. 
3. Stir in buttermilk just until flour mixture is moistened. 
4. Fold in raisins. Spoon into muffin cups. 
5. Bake 35-40 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Remove from pan.
