## Background

This is Mom's recipe.

## Ingredients

- 2 bunches broccoli tips 
- 1 lb bacon
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup (or more) finely chopped onion
- 4 tbsp cider vinegar 
- 1/2 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Fry bacon & crumble. 
2. Mix with broccoli tips.
3. In bowl mix mayonnaise, onion, cider vinegar & sugar. Add broccoli & bacon. 
4. Refrigerate 3-4 hours.
