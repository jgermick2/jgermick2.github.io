## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar 
- 2/3 cup peanut butter
- ½ cup corn syrup
- ½ cup melted butter 
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 3 cup rolled oats
- ½ cup coconut
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup wheat germ
- ½ cup chocolate chips

## Directions

1. Mix first five ingredients. Stir in dry ingredients.
2. Bake in greased 9X13 inch pan at 350⁰ for 20-25 minutes. 
3. Cool then cut.
