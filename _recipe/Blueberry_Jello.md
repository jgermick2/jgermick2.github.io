## Ingredients

- 2 small boxes Grape Jello 
- 1 can Blueberry Pie Filling
- 1 can Crushed Pineapple with Juice
- 8 oz. Cream Cheese 
- 1 cup powdered sugar 
- 8 oz. Sour Cream
- 2 1/2 oz. chopped Pecans

## Directions

1. Dissolve jello in 2 cups boiling water. 
2. Add pie filling and pineapple. 
3. Pour into a dish and cool till thick.
4. Mix cream cheese till fluffy and to it add powdered sugar and then fold in sour cream. 
5. Pour this mixture on top of the jello and then top with nuts. Refrigerate.
