## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups Bisquick 
- 1/4 cup sour cream 
- 1 egg
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 pkg taco seasoning 
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 1 can Mexican style chili beans 
- Shredded cheese
- Shredded lettuce 
- Diced tomato

## Directions

1. Blend first three ingredients to form soft dough. 
2. With lightly floured hands, spread dough into sprayed casserole dish. 
3. Brown chuck of turkey with salt and onion and drain. 
4. Blend in seasoning with a little bit of water just to dissolve. 
5. Spread on crust. 
6. Next spread chili beans on top. 
7. Bake 425 for 20- 30 minutes. 
8. Top with cheese, lettuce and tomato and serve.
