## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup margarine 
- 2 Tbl. sugar
- 1 Tbl. sesame seeds 
- 2 lb. Bok Choy
- 5 green onions
- 2 pkg. Ramen Soup Noodles (w/o seasoning)
- 5 oz. slivered almonds

Dressing:
- 3/4 cup canola oil
- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar 
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 Tbl. soy sauce

## Directions

1. In a skillet, melt margarine and add almonds, broken up noodles, sesame seeds & sugar. 
2. Cook until brown, and continue to break up noodles well. 
3. Cool down, but don't refrigerate this mixture.
4. Chop up Bok Choy and green onions together.
5. Mix dressing ingredients together. Then mix everything together when you're ready to serve.

TIP: Everything can be prepared as much as 3 days in advance, if you keep it separate (greens, dressing, skillet mixture) until ready to serve. The skillet mixture cannot be refrigerated - keep in a container on the counter.
