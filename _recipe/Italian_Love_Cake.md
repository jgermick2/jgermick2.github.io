## Background

This is Nancy Stranczek's recipe.

## Ingredients

1 box fudge marble cake mix
(2) 15 oz tubs ricotta cheese 
3/4 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 (3 3/4 oz) pkg instant pudding (milk chocolate or your choice) 1 cup milk
8 oz carton whipped topping

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 
2. Grease & flour and 13"x9" pan. Mix cake as directed on package. Pour into pan. Combine ricotta, sugar, eggs & vanilla; mix well. Spoon over unBaked cake. 
3. Bake for 1 hour; let cool. Mix puddinjg with milk, fold in whipped topping; spread over cake. 
4. Cover with plastic wrap or foil and store in the refrigerator.

It is best to use a deep 9"x13" pan.

This cake is even better a day or two after making, if there is any left over.
