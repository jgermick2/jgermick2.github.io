## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups (3 sticks) butter, softened 
- 3/4 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3 cups flour
- 1 3/4 cups (10 oz pkg) Nestle Holiday Shapes & Morsels (or any chocolate morsels)
- 1/2 cup finely chopped nuts 
- 1 sprinkle powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375 deg. Beat butter, sugar, vanilla extract and salt in large mixer bowl until creamy. Gradually beat in flour; stir in morsels and nuts. 
2. Shape level tablespoons of dough into 1 1/4 in balls. Place on ungreased baking sheets.
3. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until cookies on baking sheets. Cool on baking sheets for 10 minutes; 
4. remove to wire racks to cool completely. Sprinkle with additional powdered sugar, if desired. Store in airtight containers.
