## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour leveled before shifting 
- 2 level tsp Baking powder
- 1 level tsp Baking soda 
- 1/2 tsp caraway seeds 
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 cup raisins (heaping) 
- 3 tbs butter, melted
- 1 level tsp cream of tartar 
- 3 cups buttermilk
- 4 tbs sugar

## Directions

1. Sift flour, baking soda, baking powder, cream of tartar & salt. Add caraway seed, raisins, sugar, melted butter and mix well, before adding milk. 
2. Add 2 cups milk & toss around. Then add remaining cup of milk & mix well. Pour in greased pan. (I use my heavy frypan). Then sprinkle a tsp of plain milk & smooth off. 
3. Bake 15 minutes at 425 degrees & about 30 minutes at 400 degrees.
