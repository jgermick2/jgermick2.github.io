## Ingredients

- 12 slices bread
- 8 eggs
- 4 c. milk
- 1 lb. shredded cheddar 
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut crusts off bread. Lay bread on bottom of lightly greased, 9x13 pan. Cover with cheese. Mix eggs and milk. 
2. Pour over all and refrigerate overnight. Dot with butter. 
3 .Bake at 350 for 45 min. or until cheese browns.
