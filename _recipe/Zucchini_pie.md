## Ingredients

4 cups Shredded Zucchini 
1 cup Chopped Onion 
1/4 cup Melted Butter
2 Tbl. Parsley
1/4 tsp. Garlic Powder 
1/4 tsp. Basil
1/4 tsp. Oregano
8 oz pkg. Pillsbury Crescent Rolls 
8 oz. Shredded Mozzarella Cheese 
2 Eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Saute first seven ingredients. 
2. Then combine with beaten eggs and cheese. 
3. Pour all into a greased 10 in" pie pan that has been lined with the pkg. of Crescent Rolls. 
4. Bake @ 325 for 30 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before cutting.

If doubled, this will fit into a 9" X 13" pan.
