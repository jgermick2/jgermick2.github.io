## Ingredients

- 2 extra-ripe, medium bananas, peeled
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup packed brown sugar 
- 1/2 margarine, melted
- 1 cup blueberries
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 1/4 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon 
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Puree bananas in blender (1 cup). 
2. In medium bowl, combine bananas, eggs sugar and margarine until well blended. Stir in blueberries and vanilla. 
3. In large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until evenly moistened.
4. Spoon batter into well greased 2 1/2-inch muffin cups. 
5. Bake in 350 deg oven 25-30 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Serve warm.
