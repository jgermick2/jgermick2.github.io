## Ingredients

- 1 to 1 1/2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
- 1/4 cup margarine or butter 
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1 3/4 cups milk
- 8 ounces cheese, any type, shredded Crushed cracker crumbs (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni. 
2. Melt butter, add flour, salt and pepper. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is smooth and bubbly; remove from heat. Stir in milk. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute; remove from heat. Stir in cheese until melted.
3. Place macaroni in ungreased 1 1/2 quart casserole. Stir cheese sauce into macaroni. Top with cracker crumbs (optional). 
4. Bake uncovered in 375 degree oven 30 minutes.
