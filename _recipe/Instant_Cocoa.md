## Background

This worked really well when we went camping with Girl Scouts.

## Ingredients

- 1 box (8 qt.size) powdered milk Jar of powdered coffee creamer (8 oz.)
- 1 cup powdered sugar 
- 1 box Nestles Quick
- 1 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Mix and store in large container.	
2. Add hot water to 1/3 - 1/2 cup of mixture.
