## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking soda 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup shortening 
- 1/2 cup peanut butter 
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp milk
- 1 tbsp vanilla
- 48 candy kisses

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients. Roll into balls. Roll balls into sugar.
2. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 375-degrees 10-12 min.
3. Put kiss on top of cookie while hot.
