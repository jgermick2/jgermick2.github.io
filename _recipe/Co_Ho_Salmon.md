## Ingredients

- Whole salmon , skinned. 
- salt
- flour
- 2 sticks butter

## Directions

1. Remove skin and fat vein from top of fish. 
2. Towel dry. Salt if desired. Roll in flour. Shake off all excess flour. 
3. Melt 2 sticks of butter in shallow baking pan. 
4. Place fish in butter, belly down. 
5. Position fish so it does not lay on its sides but sits up. 
6. Bake at 350 for 45 min, basting with butter in pan every 5 min.
