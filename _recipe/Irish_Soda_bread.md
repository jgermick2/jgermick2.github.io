## Background

Mom taught me to make a cross in the batter before baking.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour 
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder 
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1 tbsp caraway seeds
- 1 cup raisins & 1 tbsp flour 
- 1/4 cup melted butter

## Directions

1. Use a large mixing bowl & sift 1st 5 ingredients. In a separate bowl beat egg & buttermilk. Add liquid to dry. 
2. Fold until no dry particles remain. Mix raisins & tbsp of flour. Fold in caraway seeds & raisins, mix in melted butter. 
3. Grease 8 x 5 x 3 loaf pan. Bake @ 325 degrees 60 - 70 minutes. Toothpick test.
