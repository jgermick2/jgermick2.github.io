## Ingredients

- 2 cans artichokes, drained
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
- 1 cup mayo
- Dash each: Garlic Salt, Worcestershire, Tobasco sauce 
- 4 oz. can green chilies, drained

## Directions

Combine all and bake @ 350 for 20 minutes. Serve with crackers.
