## Ingredients

- 1 pound checken livers
- 1/3 cup chopped onion 
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 8 to 10 drops Tabasco sauce 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp dry mustard dash of pepper

## Directions

1. In heavy skillet, cook livers & onions in 2 tbsp butter, covered over medium high heat for 5 to 8 minutes or until livers ar no longer pink. Stir occasionally. 
2. Drain and reserve 3 tbsp liquid.
3. Process liver, onions and reserved liquid in food processor or blender until finely chopped.
4. Add mayonnaise, another 2 tbsp butter, lemon juice, Tabasco, salt, mustard and pepper. Process until smooth.
5. Pour into bowl, cover & chill at least 6 hours or overnight. Serve with assorted crackers. Makes about 1 1/2 cups of pate.
