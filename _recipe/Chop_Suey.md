## Ingredients

- 1 slice of round steak
- 3 or 4 pork butts or veal cut in cubes, fat trimmed
- 6 stalks celery cut in bite size pieces 
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1 stick margarine
- 6 heaping tbsp flour
- 1 can drained sliced water chestnuts 
- 1 large bottle sliced mushrooms ( do not drain)
- 1/4 bottle of bead molasses 
- 1 can of chowmein noodles cooked rice

## Directions

1. Cook celery pieces in enough water to cover, set aside. 
2. Brown steak and other meat until tender (low heat). Add onion & cook until onion is tender. 
3. Remove meat & onion from fry pan. In same fry pan, melt 1 stick of margarine. Add 6 heaping tbsp flour, stir until blended. Add hot water from sink slowly while stirring with whisk until blended. Do not add too much water because some can be added later. 
4. Add cooked celery and water that the celery was cooked in to gravy. Add meat, mushroom & water chestnuts. 
5. At this point cook your gravy to thicken. If too thick, add a little water. If too thin, stir in small cup, 2 tbsp of cornstarch in a little cold water and add to gravy. 
6. Add soy sauce molasses to flavor. 
7. Add slowly, do not make too dark because the gravy may become bitter.
8. Serve with rice & Chowmein noodles.
