## Background

I invented this recipe in my "Atkins diet" days. (Almost carb free!) but it became a family favorite. Not exactly "diet food" if you aren't on a low carb diet but you can't go wrong with cream cheese and mozzerella! They are very filling and I often use them as a main meal. Sometimes I saute a bit of celery and spinach to add to the cream cheese. A few bread crumbs (mixed with a bit of butter) on top are nice if carbs aren't an issue.

## Ingredients

- 6 large portobello mushrooms 
- 16 oz ground sausage, browned 
- Olive oil
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese 
- 1/4 c. Parmesan cheese 
- 1/2 tsp dried mustard
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder 
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Lightly saute mushrooms in olive oil. 
2. Place on baking sheet. 
3. Combine sausage, cream cheese, mustard, and garlic powder. Scoop onto mushrooms. Top with mozzerella, parmesan, and parsley. 
4. Bake at 375 until cheese is bubbly.
