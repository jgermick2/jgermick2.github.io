## Ingredients

- 2 med. Zucchini
- 3-4 boneless chicken breasts 
- 1 can cream of celery soup 
- 1 tsp paprika
- olive oil
- 1/2 tsp basil 
- 1 clove garlic
- 1/2 cup drained diced tomatoes

## Directions

1. Chop zucchini is large pieces. 
2. Chop chicken, stir fry size. 
3. Saute chicken and garlic in a small amount of olive oil. Add remaining ingredients and cook for 10-15 min. Serve over noodles, rice, or potatoes.
