## Background

This recipe is from Granda Vania. Delores Mold's mother. She got it when she lived in the Harbor (East Chicago, IN).

## Ingredients

Pork loin roast 
diced onion
diced green pepper
taco seasoning mix
2 tbsp El Popular Mole

## Directions

1. Cook pork loin roast for 1 1/2 hours in water on top of stove. Cool & dice meat.
2. add to diced meat: a little water, diced onion & diced green pepper. Add a package of taco seasoning mix, 2 tbsp of "El Popular Mole". 
3. Cook until meat & ingredients are tender. Add water if too dry.  Cook about 1 hour.
