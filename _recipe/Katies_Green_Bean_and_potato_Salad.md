## Ingredients

- 1 lb. bacon, fried and chopped 
- 1 big purple onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 5 lb. red potatoes, boiled & cut up, not peeled
- 2 lb. fresh green beans, boiled & uncut
- Kraft Zesty Italian Dressing 
- salt & pepper, to taste

## Directions

1. Fry bacon with garlic & onion. 
2. Toss together still warm green beans and cut-up potatoes & pour bacon, garlic & onion mixture on top. 
3. Add dressing and seasoning and mix together well. Serve warm.
