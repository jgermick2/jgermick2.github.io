## Ingredients

- 2 c. sugar
- 7 c. water
- 2 c. additional water 
- 4 tea bags
- 12 oz can frozen orange juice 
- 12 oz can frozen lemonade 
- 2c. brandy

## Directions

1. Bring sugar and water to a boil. Cool. Boil additional water and add tea bags. Steep, remove bags, and cool.
2. Combine cooled liquid with orange juice, lemonade, and brandy. Freeze for
3. 12-24 hours, stir, and freeze again for 12 hours. Scoop into glass and pour lemon lime soda over slush.
