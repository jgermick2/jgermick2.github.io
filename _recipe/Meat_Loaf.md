## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs ground beef 
- 1/2 lb ground pork
- 1 small onion, grated
- 1 cup fine dry bread or cracker crumbs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten 
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients. 
2. Pack in greased loaf pan and bake in 350 degree oven about 1 1/2 hours.
