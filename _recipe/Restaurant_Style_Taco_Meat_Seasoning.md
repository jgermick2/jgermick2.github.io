## Background

This is a great way to avoid buying those taco seasoning packets - and it tastes just like the tacos from Taco Bell!

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp corn flour
- 4 1/2 tsps chili powder 
- 1/2 tsp onion powder 
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder 
- 1/2 tsp seasoned salt 
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garlic salt 
- 1/4 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp dried minced onion
- 1/2 tsp beef bouillon granules 
- 1/4 tsp ground red pepper
- 1 1/3 lb lean ground beef 
- 1 cup water

## Directions

1. In a medium bowl, combine the corn flour, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder and seasoned salt. 
2. Stir in the paprika, cumin, garlic salt, and sugar. 
3. Blend in the onion, bouillon and ground red pepper, stirring until all spices are blended. 
4. Crumble the ground beef into a large skillet over medium heat. Cook, stirring until browned. 
5. Remove from heat, rinse meat with hot water, and drain water and grease from beef.
6. Return meat to skillet and pour in seasoning. 
7. Stir in water. 
8. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until most of the liquid has cooked away, about 20 minutes.
