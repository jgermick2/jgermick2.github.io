## Background

This sauce is great for canning or freezing. It was shared by Beth's good friend, Julia.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 bushel tomatoes 
- 6 garlic cloves
- 6 medium onions
- 3 tbsp parsley
- 1/2 c. brown sugar 
- 1/4 c. salt
- 4 or 5 green peppers
- 1 bunch celery
- 3 tbsp oregano
- 3 tbsp basil
- 1 tsp pepper
- 5-6 bay leaves (whole - remove wehn serving)

## Directions

Cook tomatoes and strain. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 4-5 hours or until desired thickness is reached.
