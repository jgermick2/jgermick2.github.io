## Ingredients

- 1 bag Ore Ida Potato Wedges - Plain or Texas (spicier) 
- 3/4 lb. bacon, chopped & fried up
- 8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese 
- 4-5 green onions, diced 
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Bake potatoes as directed (can be done the day before, if desired). 
2. Arrange on oven-safe stoneware plate or a metal tray, in a circle. 
3. Sprinkle cheese, bacon, & green onions on top. 
4. Bake in oven (or microwave is OK) till cheese is melted. 
5. Serve with sour cream on the side.
