## Ingredients

- 1 bag (12-16 oz) Plain Oyster Crackers
- 1 pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch Buttermilk Recipe Salads Dressing Mix
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp. lemon pepper 
- 3/4 - 1 cup salad oil

## Directions

Combine all but crackers. Pour over crackers, stir to coat. Place in warm oven for 15-20 minutes.
