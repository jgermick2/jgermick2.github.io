## Ingredients

- 1 can Whole Kernel Corn, drained 
- 1 can Cream Style Corn
- 1 box Jiffy Corn Bread Mix 
- 1 stick melted Butter
- 8 oz. Sour Cream

## Directions

1. Mix all together & bake @ 350 for 1 hour.
