## Ingredients

- 1 pkg. Kluski Noodles, cooked
- 1 large can of Sauerkraut, drained 
- 1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
- 1 can Water
- 1 pkg. Onion Soup Mix 
- 3 Pork Steaks

## Directions

1. Brown meat. Remove from pan & cut into bite-sized pieces. 
2. Then in pan combine water, mushroom soup, and dried onion soup mix and bring all to boil. 
3. Into casserole dish, layer sauerkraut, cooked noodles, meat, and soup mixture. 
4. Repeat layers till all is used up, ending with sauerkraut. 
5. Bake covered for 1 hour @ 350.
