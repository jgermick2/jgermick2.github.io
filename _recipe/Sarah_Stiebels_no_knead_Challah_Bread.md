## Ingredients

- 1 tsp sugar
- 2 pkg dry yeast 
- 1/2 cup water
- 8 cups unsifted flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup shortening, melted or oil 
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups warm water

Egg Wash 
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup water
- caraway, poppy, or sesame seeds, optional

## Directions

1. Combine sugar, yeast and water. Allow to stand. In another bowl, mix flour, salt, shortening, sugar, egs, and warm water. Mix well and add first mixture until it is a sticky mass. Set aside for three hours. 
2. Every half-hour stir with wooden spoon. Do not cover.
3. Turn out on well-floured board. Divide into 3 parts. Shape into challah (braid). Set into three 5 x 9 greased and lightly floured Pyrex pans. Allow to rise until finger depression olds. Brush with egg water and seeds. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes.
