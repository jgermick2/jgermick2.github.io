## Ingredients

- 6 tbs butter 
- 2/3 c sugar 
- 2 eggs
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed 
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 2/3 c flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 c chopped walnuts 

Topping:
- 1/4 c firmly packed brown sugar 
- 1/4 c chopped walnuts
- 2 tbs butter, softened

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Spray pan with oil. 
2. In small bowl, combine topping ingredients. Mix to resemble course crumbs. 
3. In large bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs, bananas, and vanilla; mix well. 
4. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Add to butter mixture; mix until just blended (do not over mix.) Stir in walnuts. Pour into greased pan. Sprinkle topping evenly over batter. 
5. Bake 50-60 min or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan on cooling rack 15 min; remove from pan and cool completely.
