## Background

Mom never had a written recipe for this dish. She thought her Mom's (Joan Carmody) recipe was too bland. She thought Dad's Mom's (Rose Germick) recipe was too rich. So, she combined the two. (I'm sure it was a compromise to please Dad as well).

I believe the secret is to have plenty of sauce. It's just a white sauce recipe and you add Cheese Whiz. I would probably make 3 cups of sauce for a pound of noodles & maybe 1 1/2 containers of cottage cheese. (If this is too much sauce, you use the remainder for something else). Add Cheese Whiz to taste. I would probably use 2 jars for 3 cups of white sauce. But if I tasted it & thought it needed more cheese, I'd add more cheese. I use more Cheese Whiz than Mom did. She loved my Macaroni & Cheese but said it was too rich & that I should only make it once a year.

Diane uses an egg beater and blends the cheese sauce with the cottage cheese. She says she gets a better blending of the two cheeses that way.

I believe you need to cook it at least 1 1/2 hours. You need the cottage cheese to melt.

## Ingredients

- 3 tbs butter 
- 1/4 c. flour 
- 1 c. milk
- 1 jar cheez whiz 
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 16 oz cottage cheese
- 1 pkg macaroni noodles, prepared according to directions
- 1 sleeve crackers, crushed paprika

## Directions

1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter. 
2. Add flour and cook 1 minute. Slowly add milk, stirring constantly. 
3. Add Cheese Whiz and pepper.

4. Layer noodles, cheese sauce, and cottage cheese, ending with a layer of cheese sauce. 
5. Top with cracker crumbs and paprika. 
6. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
