## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 2 tbsp chopped onion 
- 1 tbsp chili powder 
- 1/4 tsp ground cumin 
- 1/4 tsp oregano
- 1 lrg tomato diced
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar tabasco to taste
- 1/2 head lettuce
- 1 1/2 cup crushed Doritos 
- 1/2 cup black olives, sliced 
- 1 avocado (opt)
- 3 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 1/2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook beef and onion. Stir in chili powder, cumin, oregano, Half tomato, balsamic vinegar and tabasco. Cook 3-5 minutes. Drain off fat. Cool (if desired)

2. Shred lettuce. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese, crushed chips and olives. Stir in avocado and other half of tomato.

3. Add meat mixture. Drizzle olive oil and 1 1/2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar over and toss.
