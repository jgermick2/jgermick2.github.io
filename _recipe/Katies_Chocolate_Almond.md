## Background

I had this in my recipe box as a recipe from Katie. She says she never saw this recipe before. So who knows where it came from.

## Ingredients

- 2 c. flour
- 1 c. sugar
- 1/3 c. cocoa powder 
- 1 tsp baking soda 
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg whites
- 3/4 tsp vanilla extract
- 2/3 c. whole almonds, toasted 
- 1/3 c. chocolate chips
- 8 oz almond bark, melted

## Directions

1. Combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, soda and salt. Toss almonds with flour mixture. Mix together eggs and whites and vanilla. Add egg mixture to flour and blend til combined.
2. On floured surface, roll dough into 2 logs, 2 in diameter. Place on greased sheet pan and brush with beaten egg.
3. Bake at 350 until golden brown (30-35 min). Allow logs to cool 15 min. then cut into slices on the bias. 
4. Place slices on a greased sheet pan and bake in 350 oven until toasted 15-20 min. 
5. Cool. Dip in almond bark.
