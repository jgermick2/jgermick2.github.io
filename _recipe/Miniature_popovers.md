## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour 
- 3 tbsp butter, melted, divided 
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt 
- 4 large eggs
- 1/2 cup honey

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 450 deg. Grease 30 minature muffin cups. 
2. Combine milk, flour 1 1/2 tbsp butter, cinnamon and salt in a large bowl and mix just until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time. Do not over-beat; batter should be consistancy of whipping cream.
3. Fill muffin cups three-fourths full with batter. 
4. Bake for 7 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350. Bake popovers until golden, about 8-10 minutes.
5. Combine honey and remaining butter in a small bowl and mix well. Drizzle over hot popovers. Serve immediately.
