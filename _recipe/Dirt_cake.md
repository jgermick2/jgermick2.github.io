## Ingredients

- 20 oz. Oreo Cookies 
- 3/4 stick of Margarine 
- 8 oz. Cream Cheese
- 1/3 cup Powdered Sugar 
- 4 cups Milk
- 12 oz. Cool Whip
- 2 sm. boxes instant pudding, French Vanilla OR Milk Chocolate

Plastic flower pot, plastic flowers, gummi worms, sand shovel, etc.

## Directions

1. Crush cookies on waxed paper and set aside.
2. Cream together: margarine, cream cheese, & sugar.
3. Beat milk & pudding for 2 minutes and add in cool whip until thick. Then blend cream cheese mixture into pudding mixture.
4. Layer oreos, cream, oreos, cream (ending with oreos) into a big, new, plastic flowerpot. Refrigerate.
5. I decorated with a bunch of plastic flowers & gummi worms.
