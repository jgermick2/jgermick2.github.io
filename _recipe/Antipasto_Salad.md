## Ingredients

- 1 box twisted rotini, drained & still hot
- 1 chopped green pepper 
- 1 chopped purple onion
- 1 can medium-sized black olives, drained & sliced
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved 
- 1 package pepperoni, halved 
- 1/2 bottle Zesty Italian dressing 
- 8 oz. cheddar cheese, cubed

## Directions

1. Mix all together EXCEPT for the cheese (or it will melt) and chill. Add cheese and more dressing, to taste.
