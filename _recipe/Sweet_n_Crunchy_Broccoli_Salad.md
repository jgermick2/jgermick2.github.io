## Ingredients

- 2 bunches Broccoli, chopped bite-sized
- 1/2 lb. Bacon, fry & chop 
- 1/2 cup Red Onion, diced 
- 1 cup Raisins
- 1/2 cup Sunflower Seeds 
- 1 cup Cashews

Dressing:
- 1 cup Mayo 
- 1/4 cup Sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp. White Vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix dressing ingredients till smooth. 
2. Then stir into remaining ingredients.
