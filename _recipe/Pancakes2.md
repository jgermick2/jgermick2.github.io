## Ingredients

3 T. butter, melted 
3 T. sugar
1 egg
3/4 tsp. salt
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder 
1 1/4 cup flour
3/4 cup milk

## Directions

Mix together the butter and sugar. Add egg and mix. Add salt, baking powder and flour. Gradually add milk; add more if necessary.
