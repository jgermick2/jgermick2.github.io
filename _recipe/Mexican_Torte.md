## Background

This is Maureen Mulholland's recipe

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup chopped onion 
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1-2 cloves garlic 
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 can tomato sauce
- chili powder & cumin (to taste) salt & pepper
- flour tortillas
- 1 cup grated cheese 
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/2 cup finely chopped onion 
- 1 pkg frozen chopped spinach, drained
- black olives
- refried beans (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook onions & minced garlic in oil until transparent, but not brown. Add meat & brown. Add tomato sauce & spices. 
2. Simmer 30 minutes. 
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spoon a little sauce over bottom of greased casserole & cover with tortillas (overlap if necessary).
4. Layer with cheese, onion, spinach, (beans), & sauce, separating each layer with tortillas. 
5. Continue until all ingredients are used ending with layer of sauce. S
6. poon a dollop of sour cream in the middle & garnish with olives. 
7. Bake about 20 minutes until bubbly.
8. Serve piping hot.
