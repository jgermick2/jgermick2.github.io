## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Hamburger, browned & drained
- 1 lb. Bacon, crisped & crumbled
- 2 small Onions, chopped & sauteed
- 1 can Kidney Beans
- 1 large can Pork & Beans
- 1 1/2 cup Light Brown Sugar 
- 1 cup Catsup
- 4 tsp. Mustard
- 4 tsp. Vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together. 
2. Put into casserole dish and bake at 350 for 1 hour.
