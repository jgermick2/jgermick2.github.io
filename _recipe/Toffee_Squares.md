## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar 
- 1 egg
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 1 cup nuts
- 8 oz. milk chocolate

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar. Add beaten egg. Add vanilla and flour.
2. Bake at 350 deg. for 15-20 minutes in 10x15 pan.
3. Spread with chocolate and nuts while warm.
