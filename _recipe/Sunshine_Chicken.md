## Background

Obviously I got this recipe off a box of Uncle Ben's Converted Rice, but it's good and different.

## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 tsp curry powder 
- 1 1/4 tsp salt (optional) 
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 6 chicken breast halves, boned & skinned
- 1 1/2 cups orange juice
- 1 cup Uncle Ben's converted brand rice
- 3/4 cup water
- 1 tbsp brown sugar 
- 1 tsp dry mustard chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Combine curry powder, 1/2 tsp salt and pepper. 
2. Sprinkle over chicken; rub seasonings onto top and bottom surfaces of each chicken piece and set aside. 
3. In 10-inch skillet, combine orange juice, rice, water, brown sugar, mustard & remaining 3/4 tsp salt; mix well. 
4. Arrange chicken pieces over rice. Bring to boil. Cover & simmer 20 minutes. 
5. Remove from heat & let stand covered until liquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes. 
6. Sprinkle with parsley.
