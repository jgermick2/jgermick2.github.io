## Ingredients

- 2 pkg. Crescent Rolls
- 2 - 8 oz. Cream Cheese
- 1 cup Sugar
- 1 tsp. Vanilla
- 1 Egg YOLK
- 1 Egg WHITE

Topping:
- 1/2 cup Sugar
- 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped 
- 1 tsp. Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Press 1 pkg. rolls onto bottom of greased 9 X 13 pan.

2. Beat together remaining ingredients on left, EXCEPT for the egg white and pour onto crescent roll bottom. Roll out 2nd pkg. rolls and lay onto cheese mixture.

3. Beat egg white to foam and paint onto dough.

4. Mix topping ingredients together & sprinkle over top. Bake @ 350 for 30 minutes.
