## Ingredients

- 2 cups sifted flour
- 1/2 cup confectioners sugar 
- 1 cup butter

Filling:
- 2- 8 oz pkg cream cheese
- 1 - 7 oz jar marshmallow cream
- 1 carton (4 1/2 oz) frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 2 cans (21 oz each) cherry pie filling

## Directions

1. Sift flour with confectioners sugar. Cut in butter. Press into a jelly roll pan (15 x 10 x 1). 
2. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 min. 
3. Mix cream cheese, marshmallow cream, and whipped topping with electric mixer until smooth. 
4. Spread on cooled crust. Top with pie filling. 
5. Refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
