## Background

This is Mom's recipe. Mom used to tell the story about Dad inviting Joy & Warren (their friends at Purdue) over to their apartment for dinner. He told them Mom would just whip up a batch of fried rice for dinner. Well since Dad invited them to dinner without talking to Mom & since he said she could just whip up a batch of fried rice, Mom let Dad make the dinner. As the story goes, Dad didn't cook the rice before he fried it. So the dinner was not edible.

I have used these same ingredients, minus the rice, plus some bean sprouts and made egg rolls.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup of cooked brown rice 
- 2 eggs fried
- Oil
- Chopped green onions 
- Sliced mushrooms (optional)
- Chopped celery 
- 1/8 tsp pepper 
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- canned shrimp or small frozen shrimp (thawed)

## Directions

1. Cook brown rice according to directions, cool. 
2. Fry eggs. As eggs are frying, break the yolk and spread over the egg whites. 
3. Cool fried eggs & dice. 
4. Saute green onions, celery & mushrooms in oil. They can be sauted in the same pan. Celery takes longer to saute than green onions & green onions takes longer to saute than mushrooms. Saute celery, add green onions & saute, then add mushrooms & saute. 
5. Add pepper & brown rice to sauted vegatables, mix and continue to fry. A
6. dd diced eggs, mix and continue to fry. 
7. Once fried rice is constantly hot, add shrimp, mix and continue to fry just to heat shrimp. 
8. Add 1 tbsp of soy sauce, mix and serve hot.
