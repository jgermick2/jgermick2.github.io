## Ingredients

3 tomatoes, chopped OR 2 cans tomatoes
½ c finely diced onion
3 jalapeno peppers OR 5 serrano chiles, finely chopped
½ c chopped cilantro OR parsley 1 tsp salt
2 tsp lime juice OR juice of 1 lime

## Directions

Stir together or process for a smoother salsa. Suggested additions:
Garlic
2 Tbsp ketchup Hot sauce Cumin
Sugar
