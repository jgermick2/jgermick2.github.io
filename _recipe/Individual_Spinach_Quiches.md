## Ingredients

- 2 crust pie pastry 
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/2 pkg frozen chopped spinach (thawed)
- 1.4 lb swiss or gruyere cheese, shredded 
- 1/2 c. half and half
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

## Directions

1. Grease and flour thirty-six mini muffin cups. 
2. Roll pastry about 1/8 in thick. Cut into 3 in rounds. 
3. Line muffin pans with pastry cirles. 
4. Brush with melted butter. 
5. Refrigerate 30 min. Preheat oven to 400. 
6. Drain spinach. Combine with remaining ingredients. 
7. Spoon about 1 tbsp egg mixture into each cup. 
8. Bake 25 min or until knife comes out clean.
