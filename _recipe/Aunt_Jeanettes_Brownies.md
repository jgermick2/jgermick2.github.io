## Background

This is a double recipe and can be cut in half.

## Ingredients

- 2 sticks margarine or butter
- 4 squares unsweetened chocolate 
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking powder 
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 generous cup of chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Cut margarine and chocolate squares into chunks and melt over low heat, stirring constantly. 
2. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, mixing well. Set aside. Grease 9x13 pan. Stir ingredients in bowl again. Add eggs, one at a time, stirring well after each addition. Stir flour and add 1 1/2 cups to batter. Add baking powder and salt. Add vanilla and nuts. 
3. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 min.
