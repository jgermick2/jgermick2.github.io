## Ingredients

- 1/2 c. confectioners sugar 
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1/2 lb butter
- 1 tsp vanilla 
- 2 1/4 c. flour 
- 1/2 cup nuts
- 1 additional cup confectioners sugar

## Directions

1. Cream together sugar, butter, and salt. Add vanilla. Stir in flour and nuts. Roll into 1 in. balls. 
2. Bake at 400 for 10-12 min.
3. While still warm, roll in additional confectioners sugar. Cool on cooling rack
