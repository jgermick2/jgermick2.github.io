## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups flour 
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup fat-free milk 
- 3 tbsp cooking oil

## Directions

1. In a medium bowl stir together flour and salt. Combine milk and cooking oil. Add milk mixture all at once to flour mixture. Stir lightly with a fork until dough forms. Form into a ball. 
2. On a lightly floured surface, slightly flatten dough. Roll dough into a 12- inch circle. 
3. Ease pastry into a 9-in pie plate. Trim pastry to 1/2 in beyond edge of plate. Fold under extra pastry; crimp edge as desired.
