## Background

This was a favorite Christmas cookie recipe passed down from Grandma Rose Reinert.

## Ingredients

- 4 1/2 sticks of butter 
- 1 c. sugar
- 1/2 c. confectioners sugar
- 1 tbs. vanilla 
- 1/2 tsp. nutmeg 
- 5 c. flour decorating sugar

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375.
2. Cream butter and both sugars. Add vanilla and nutmeg. Gradually add the flour.
3. Roll into balls. Roll balls in sugar. Flatten with bottom of juice glass dipped in sugar.
4. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet 10-12 min. Cool on cookie rack.
