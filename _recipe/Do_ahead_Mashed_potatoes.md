## Ingredients

- 4 lbs potatoes
- 8 oz cream cheese 
- 1 stick butter
- 1 cup sour cream
- salt and pepper to taste 
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder 
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 cup chives

## Directions

1. Boil and mash potatoes. 
2. Add rest of ingredients Mix together
3. Bake at 350-degrees 1/2 hour if potatoes are warm 
4. If cold: 1 - 1 1/2 hours.
5. Can make a day ahead.
