## Background

This is a great recipe for a lighter, low cal pie without a lot of heavy sugary syrup. Use with Oil Pastry for Single Crust Pie for an even lower-cal fare.
It's yummy and good for you. Use with real flavorful strawberries - the syrup doesn't cover up the yummy taste of good fresh strawberries.

## Ingredients

- 6 cups strawberries, halved 
- 1 cup water
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- Few drops of red food coloring

## Directions

1. In a blender container or food processor bowl, combine 1 cup of the strawberries and the water. Cover and blend or process until smooth. 
2. Transfer to a small saucepan. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, for 2 minutes.
3. In a medium saucepan stir together sugar and cornstarch; stir in berry mixture. Cook and stir over medium heat until tickened and bubbly. Cook and stir for 2 minutes more. Remove from heat. If desired, stir in enough red food coloring to tint a rich red color. 
4. Cool syrup mixture to room temperature.
5. Fold remaining strawberries into cooled syrup mixture; transfer to cooked pastry shell. 
6. Cover, chill for 3 to 4 hours. If desired, serve with whipped topping.
