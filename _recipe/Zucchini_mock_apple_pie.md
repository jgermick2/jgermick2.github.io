## Ingredients

Filling:
- 8 cups Zucchini; peeled, halved, seeded & cut into chunks (apple-sized)
- 3/4 cup lemon juice 
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 tsp. each - nutmeg & cinnamon

Crust:
- 2 sticks margarine
- 3 cups flour
- 1 1/2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Cook zucchini & lemon juice till tender. Add sugar & spices. Cool. (Can be frozen for later use.)

2. Mix crust ingredients together. Place HALF into 9 X 13 pan and bake for 10 minutes @ 350.

3. Pour cool zucchini mixture onto crust. Top all with other half of crust mixture. Sprinkle some cinnamon on top & bake at 350 for 40 - 50 minutes (till it looks golden, like apple pie). Good with ice cream.
