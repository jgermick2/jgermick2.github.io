## Ingredients

- 3 bunches raw, fresh broccoli tops, cut into chunks
- Cherry Tomatoes, halved
- large jar of Green Giant sliced Mushrooms, drained
- Seven Seas Viva Italian Dressing

## Directions

1. Mix together & sprinkle with dressing. 
2. Let sit for several hours. 
3. Remix and add more dressing, to taste.
