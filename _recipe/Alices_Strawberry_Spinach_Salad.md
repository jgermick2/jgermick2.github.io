## Ingredients

Dressing:
- 1/3 cup Red Wine Vinegar 
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup sugar 
- 1 cup oil
- 1 tsp dry mustard
- 1 1/2 Tbl minced onions
- 1 1/2 Tbl poppy seeds
- 1 bag spinach, washed, & drained
- 1/2 cup toasted pecans*
- 1-2 pts Fresh strawberries, (cut into 1/2 or 1/4 )

## Directions

1. Put all dressing ingredients in blender at high speed until it turns white or opaqueDon't over-blend or poppy seeds will puree
2. Toss remaining ingredients with dressing right before serving
3. Toast pecans in oven for 10 minutes @ 350 on an ungreased cookie sheet and check for crispness
