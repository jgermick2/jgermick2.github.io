## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 c. milk
- 1 pkg Budding Beef

## Directions

1. Melt butter. Add flour. Cook until thick. 
2. Add milk and cook till thick. 
3. Cut up beef and add to cream sauce.  Heat.  Serve over toast.
