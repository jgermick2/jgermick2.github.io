## Ingredients

- 4 boned chicken breasts, 6 oz each 
- 1/2 cup cooked spinach, drained
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper
- 3/4 cup tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400 F. Skin chicken breasts, cut away filet mignon from the underside of chicken breast and draw out the tendons. 
2. In a blender, finely chop filets mignons of chicken with spinach. Add the egg and eason with salt and pepper. Slit the breast and fill the cavity with stuffing.
3. Pour tomato sauce in a baking dish and add stufed chicken breasts. Cover dish with aluminum foil. Cook in oven approximately 40 minutes.
