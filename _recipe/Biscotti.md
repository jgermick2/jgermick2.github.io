## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup shortening 
- 3/4 cup white sugar 
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 4 eggs
- 2 tbsp milk
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1-2 tsp almond, lemon or anise flavor
- 4 cups flour
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1-2 cups chopped almonds or hazelnuts
- 1 tbsp anise seed, optional

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, shortening and the white and brown sugar. Add eggs, milk and flavorings, blend well. Gradually add the flour and then baking powder, mix well. Add nuts and anise seeds, if desired.

2. Shape dough into 4 rolls onto 2 greased cookie sheets. Gently flatted into long, narrow mounds that are slightly higher in the center and tapering off towards the sides. Bake at 350 deg for 30-40 minutes. Cool slightly. Cut into 1/4-1/2 in slices.

3. Bake for an additional 8-15 minutes, depending upon personal preference. Allow to cool completely and store in an airtight container.

Biscotti can be dipped in chocolate if desired.
