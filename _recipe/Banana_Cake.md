## Background

This is Aunt Mary Holman's recipe.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup mashed banana (4 medium sized - very ripe) 
- 1/4 lb butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar 
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups cake flour 
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 cup sour milk or buttermilk (1 tsp vinegar & 1/4 cup milk) 
- 1/2 cup ground or chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Cream sugar & butter. Beat egg yolks, add milk, vanilla.
2. Combine with mashed banana & beat into creamed mixture. Add sifted flour & soda. Add nuts. Beat egg whites and fold into batter. 
3. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.

note:
Double ingredients for 9" x 13" cake pan.
