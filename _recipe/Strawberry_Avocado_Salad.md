## Ingredients

1⁄4 cup balsamic vinegar 
1 cup sliced strawberries
1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
1 cup halved mini mozzarella balls 
1 ripe avocado, pitted and diced
1 cup pecans, toasted
1 cup loosely packed basil
Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling 
Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Directions

1. In a small saucepan, bring the balsamic vinegar to a high simmer over medium heat. 
2. Stir, then reduce the heat to low and simmer until the vinegar has thickened and reduced by half, about 8 to 10 minutes. 
3. Set aside to cool.

4. Place the strawberries, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, avocado, pecans and basil in a shallow bowl or platter. 
5. Drizzle with olive oil, and season generously with salt and pepper. Gently toss. 
6. Drizzle with the reduced balsamic.
