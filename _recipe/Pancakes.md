## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup flour 
- 3/4 cup milk 
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp shortening, melted or vegtable oil 
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Beat egg with hand beater until fluffy; beat in remaining ingredients just until smooth. For thinner pancakes, stir in additional 1/4 cup milk. 
2. Grease heated griddle if necessary.
3. For each pancake, pour about 3 tablespoons batter from tip of large spoon or from pitcher onto hot griddle. 
4. Cook pancakes until puffed and dry around edges. Turn and cook other sides until golden brown.
