## Ingredients

- 2 cups Captain Crunch Cereal 
- 2 cups Rice Krispies Cereal
- 2 cups Pretzel sticks
- 2 cups walnuts or pecans, chopped 
- 1 lb. white coating chocolate

## Directions

Mix top 4 ingredients together. Mix in melted coating chocolate and drop by tablespoons onto wax paper.

Methods for melting coating chocolate:

1.) DOUBLE BOILER-Heat water hot but NOT BOILING in the bottom of a double boiler (with coating nowhere near the stove). Remove water from the stove, put top of double boiler with a handful of coating in it OVER (but not touching) the water.  Don't allow top half to touch water.  Stir & add more coating, one handful at a time until each melts, approximately 20 to 25 minutes.

2.) MICROWAVE-Place coating in a micro-safe container. Place in micro at HALF POWER at 30-second intervals, stirring every 30 seconds. Coating does not lose its shape during micro melting until stirred. After melting in micro, coating may still be held over hot tap water to maintain consistency. Do not try to add solid pieces of coating to some that is melted and re- microwave. The melted candy will burn and the solid candy won't do anything.

Note: Never let water get into the chocolate. If it gets too thick, warm it over hot water.
