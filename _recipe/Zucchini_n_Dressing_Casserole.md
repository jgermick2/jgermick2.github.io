## Ingredients

- 5 medium Zucchini, cut into 1/2 in. thick moons 
- 1-2 diced onions
- 2 grated carrots
- Pepper & Sweet Basil, to taste
- 1 pkg. Stove Top Stuffing (cooked per pkg. directions) 
- 1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
- 8 oz. Grated Cheddar Cheese 
- 1 can French Fried Onions

## Directions

1. Saute vegetables. 
2. Season, and place into greased9" X 13" pan. Swirl in prepared stuffing, soup, and cheese.
3. Top with can of onions and Bake 40 minutes @ 350.
