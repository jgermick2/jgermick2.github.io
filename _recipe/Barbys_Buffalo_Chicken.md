## Ingredients

- 8 oz. Cream Cheese
- 3 oz. Hot Sauce (Buffalo sauce) 
- 4 oz. Bleu Cheese
- 1 cup Ranch dressing
- 1 lg. can chicken, drained 
- 4 oz. Grated Cheddar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients except for Cheddar together and place in a 9 X 9 pan. 
2. Top with grated Cheddar. 
3. Microwave 4 minutes OR bake 15 min. @ 350. 
4. Serve with tostitos & celery.
