## Ingredients

- 1/2 c. peanut butter 
- 1/2 c. butter
- 6 oz chocolate chips
- 10 c. corn chex cereal 
- 2 c. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Melt peanut butter, butter and chocolate chips in a sauce pan over medium heat. 
2. Pour over corn chex, stirring until all cereal is coated. 
3. Put powdered sugar in a large paper bag. Add cereal and shake until all cereal is coated.
