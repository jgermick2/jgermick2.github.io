## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground pork 
- 1 tbsp onion flakes
- 3/4 cup crushed corn flakes 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
- 1 large egg, lightly beaten 
- 1/4 cup tomato ketchup
- 3 tbsps firmly packed brown sugar 
- 1 tsp dry mustard

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375 deg F. 
2. In large bowl, combine ground pork, onion, corn flakes, salt, pepper and egg. 
3. In a small bowl, stir together ketchup, brown sugar and dry mustard. 
4. Spoon 2 tbsp of ketchup mixture into pork and mix well. Spray muffin tin with cooking spray. 
5. Form 6 meatballs and place in muffin tin. Coat top of each meatball with a spoonful of remaining ketchup mixture. 
6. Bake for 30 minutes or until done.
