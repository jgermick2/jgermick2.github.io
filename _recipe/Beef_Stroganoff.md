## Ingredients

- 3 lbs round steak, cut 
- 1/2 in thick 
- 3 tbs. butter
- 1 lrg onion
- 4 tbsp flour
- 1 cn condensed consomme
- 1 cn condensed cream of tomato soup
- 8 oz can mushrooms, stems and pieces
- 1 tsp. monosodium glutamate salt and pepper
- 1 c. thick sour cream or buttermilk 
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Cooked noodles

## Directions

1. Remove fat from meat. 
2. Cut meat across the grain in narrow strips about 2 in. long. Heat butter in a large heavy skillet. 
3. Add meat and cook, stirring with a fork until browned. Add onion. Continue cooking 2-3 minutes.
4. Sprinkle flour over the meat and stir well. Add consumme and tomato soup. 
5. Cook, stirring constantly until sauce boils and thickens. Add drained mushrooms and seasonings. 
6. Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally for 1 to 1 1/4 hours or til meat is tender. 
7. Stir in sour cream and paprika. Serve over noodles.
