## Background

This is Delores Mold's recipe. She always double the recipe when she made it.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup peanut butter regular or crunchy 
- 1/4 cup Crisco
- 1/2 cup  brown  sugar 
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar 
- 1 egg
- 1 cup all purpose flour 
- 1 tsp baking soda 
- Spanish peanuts

## Directions

1. Measure 1st 5 ingredients into large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add baking soda to flour. 
2. Add flour mixture to creamed mixture, stir with spoon until blended. 
3. Add Spanish peanuts to batter and mix. With a teaspoon, make mounds in hand. Place small formed mounds on greased cookie sheet. Press with fork.
4. Bake 10 - 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
