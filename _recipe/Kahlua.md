## Ingredients

2 cups boiling water 
1/2 cup instant coffee 
3 cups sugar
1 qt. OR fifth of vodka
1 whole vanilla bean OR   1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Dissolve coffee and sugar in boiling water and then cool. 
2. Pour mixture into 1/2 gallon jug and add vodka. 
3. Break vanilla bean into 4 0r 5 pieces and put into jug. 
4. Let stand in closed jug for 30 days, then remove beans.

If you use vanilla extract instead of beans, kahlua can be used immediately.
