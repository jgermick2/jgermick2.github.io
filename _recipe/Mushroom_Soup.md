## Ingredients

- 1 lb of mushrooms, sliced 
- 2 cans of sliced mushrooms
- 2 tbsp onion, finely chopped 
- 4 tbsp butter
- 2 1/2 cups water
- 1 1/2 cups or 12 oz chicken broth 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper (white pepper)

White sauce:
- 8 tbsp butter 
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 quart milk-whole

## Directions

1. In a kettle, saute mushrooms & onion in butter. Add water , chicken broth and juice from mushroom jars to the mushroom & onion mixture. 
2. Simmer until reduced by half. Add salt & pepper.
3. White Sauce: In separate pan, melt butter over low heat. Gradually add flour, stirring constantly. Slowly stir in milk. Cook until thickened & smooth. 
4. Slowly add white sauce to mushroom/chicken broth, stirring constantly. Simmer 30 minutes.
