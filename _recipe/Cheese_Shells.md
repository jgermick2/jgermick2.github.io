## Ingredients

- 1 lb hamburger
- 1 med. onion
- 32 oz spaghetti sauce 
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tbs sugar
- 1 box jumbo shells
- 2 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese 
- 16 oz shredded mozzarella 
- 1 tbsp parsley flakes
- 1 egg
- 1/4 c. parmesan

## Directions

1. Brown hamburger and onion. 
2. Drain. Add sauce, oregano, and sugar. 
3. Cook shells according to directions on package. 
4. In separate bowl, blend cream cheese, 8 oz mozzarella, parsley, egg, and parmasan.
5. Spoon 1 c. sauce on bottom of pan. 
6. Fill shells with cheese mixture. 
7. Top with remaining sauce and remaining mozzarella cheese. Bake 45 min at 350.
