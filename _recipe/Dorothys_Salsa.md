## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped tomatoes 
- 1 cup chopped cucumbers 
- 1 cup chopped red onions
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- Coarse salt, pepper and basil to taste

## Directions

1. Mix and refrigerate.
2. Slice Italian bread at angle. 
3. Toast under broiler. Brush with olive oil. Top toasted bread with mixture, sprinkle with grated cheese.

OR Serve mixture as a salad.  Add bread cubes to salad.
