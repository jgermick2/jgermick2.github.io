## Ingredients

- 8 oz sour cream
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1/2 pkg taco seasoning 
- 2 c. chopped lettuce
- 8 oz cheddar cheese
- 1 green pepper, 
- chopped 1 large tomato, chopped
- 1 sm can sliced black olives

## Directions

1. Beat sour cream, cream cheese, and taco seasoning together. 2. Spread on platter. 
3. Top with veggies and cheese.
