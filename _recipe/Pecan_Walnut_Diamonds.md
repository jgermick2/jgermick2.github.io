## Ingredients

Crust:
- 1 1/2 cups sifted all-purpose flour 
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 3/4 brown sugar, firmly packed
- 1/2 cup butter, cut into six pieces (butter should be cold)

Topping:
- 1/4 cup light corn syrup 
- 6 oz butterscotch morsels 
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp water
- 7 oz (2 cups) walnuts and pecans

## Directions

Crust:
1. Preheat oven to 375 deg. 
2. Combine all ingredients in the bowl of a food processor. Pulse on and off until all ingrediants are well combined. (A pastry blender can also be used). 
3. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of an ungreased 9 x 13 inch pan. Press firmly to make a smooth compact layer.
4. Bake the crust on the bottom rack of your oven for 12 minutes or until it is lightly browned.

Topping:
1. Place all ingrediants, except nuts, in the top of a double boiler over hot water on medium heat. 
2. Stir constantly with a rubber spatuala, pressing against the morsels, until they are melted and the mixture is smooth. 
3. Stir in the nuts and pour mixture over the Baked crust. Spread evenly over crust. 
4. Return to the 375 deg oven and bake for exactly 10 minutes. 
5. Let cookies cool in the pan for 1 hour or more. Cut into diamonds or squares. Store in an airtight container for up to a week or in the freezer.
