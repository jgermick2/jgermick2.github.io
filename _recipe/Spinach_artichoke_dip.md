## Background

Gotta love those Pampered Chef recipes! Depending on the brand, some marinated artichoke hearts have a bit too much seasoning. I often rinse them a bit to lighten the taste.

## Ingredients

- 1 jar marinated artichoke hearts
- 1 pkg frozen spinach, thawed and drained 
- 1 pressed garlic clove
- 1/2 c. sour cream 
- 1/2 c. mayo
- 3/4 c. grated parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375. 
2. Drain and chop artichokes. Mix with remaining ingredients. 
3. Spoon into small baking pan. 
4. Bake 20-25 minutes.
