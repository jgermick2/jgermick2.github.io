A couple of us entered this recipe - the only difference being some mix then poppy seeds into sauce and spread the sauce on the inside of the sandwich instead of the top.

## Ingredients

- 24 slider rolls (mini Hawaiian are good)
- 24 slides Honey Ham 24 slices Swiss cheese Mayo

Sauce:
- 1 1/2 Tbsp Yellow Mustard 
- 8 Tbsp Butter, melted
- 1 Tbsp finely minced onion (or 2 tsp dried minced)
- 1/2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce Poppy Seeds, for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Split rolls and spread insides lightly with mayo. 
2. Fold a slice of ham to fit on each bun, and top with a slice of Swiss cheese. 
3. Replace the top bun to assemble the sandwiches. Place on a baking sheet.

To make sauce: 
1. Combine all ingredients, except the poppy seeds, in a small bowl and whisk to combine. 
2. Drizzle sauce over the tops of the slider buns. Sprinkle with poppy seeds. 
3. Cover with foil and bake 10 minutes @ 350, until the cheese is melted. 
4. Remove the foil and bake 2 minutes longer. Serve warm.
