## Ingredients

- 2 tsp Chili Powder
- 1 tsp Ground Cumin 
- 1 tsp Minced Garlic 
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 cup chopped onion 
- 2/8 oz cans tomato sauce
- 15 oz can black beans, drained & rinsed
- 1 can (8 3/4 oz) corn, whole kernel, undrained
- 1 can (4 1/2 oz) chopped green chilies, undrained 
- 1/2 tsp Oregano Leaves
- 1/2 tsp Crushed Red Pepper 
- 6 flour tortillas (8 inch)
- 2 c shredded Cheddar Cheese

## Directions

1. Brown beef with onion and drain. 
2. Add tomato sauce, beans, corn & green chilies; mix well. Stir in all spices except Red Pepper. 
3. Bring to a boil Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes. Add Red Pepper to taste, if desired.
4. Spread 1/2 cup of the beef mixture on bottom of 13X9 baking dish, sprayed with Pam. 
5. Top with 3 of the tortillas, overlapping as needed. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese. 
6. Repeat with remaining tortillas, beef mixture and cheese. 
7. Bake @ 350 for 15 minutes or till heated through. 
8. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
