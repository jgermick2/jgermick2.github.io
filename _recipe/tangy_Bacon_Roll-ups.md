## Ingredients

- 2 cans whole water chestnuts, drained
- 1 pkg fully cooked bacon slices 
- 1/2 c. packed brown sugar
- 1/2 c. ketchup
- 1 tbsp cider vinegar 
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 2 garlic cloves, pressed

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400. 
2. Line pan with parchment paper. 
3. Cut bacon crosswise in half. 
4. Wrap bacon slice around each chestnut; 
5. secure with a toothpick.
6. In a small saucepan, combine remaining ingredients. 
7. Boil, reduce heat, and cook 5 min. 
8. Dip each chestnut into sauce; tap to remove excess, and place on pan. 
9. Bake 18-20 min. or until sauce is thick and darkened.
