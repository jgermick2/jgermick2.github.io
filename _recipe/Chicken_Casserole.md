## Ingredients

- 4 chicken breasts, diced
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup 
- 1 can evap. milk
- 1/2 c. regular milk
- 1 can drained mushrooms 
- 1 pkg onion soup mix
- 1 c. raw rice

## Directions

1. Mix above ingredients in bowl. 
2. Arrange chicken in 9x13 in. pan. Pour over chicken. 
3. Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours.
