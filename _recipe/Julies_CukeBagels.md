## Ingredients

- 2/8 oz Cream Cheese, 
- softened 1 Tbl Mayo
- 1 pkg Hidden Valley Ranch mix 2 pkg Bagels (10 or so?)
- 2 lg Cukes, peeled & sliced

## Directions

1. Blend together first 3 ingredients. 
2. Toast bagels in oven - inside halves UP - for 15 minutes @ 350. 
3. Break bagels into 8 hunks and smear on the cream cheese mixture. 
4. Top with Cukes and serve. Delish!
