## Ingredients

- 2 c. brown sugar 
- 2 sticks butter
- 1/2 c. white syrup 
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp butter flavoring 
- 1/2 tsp soda
- 8 qts popped corn

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients except soda and corn. Boil 5 min. Turn off heat. 
2. Add soda. 
3. Pour over corn and stir well. 
4. Put in pans. Bake at 250 for 1 hour, stirring every 15 min. 
5. Cool and store.
