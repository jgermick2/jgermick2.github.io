## Ingredients

- 4 tbsp butter
- 1/2 c. chopped onion 
- 3/4 c. chopped celery
- 1/2 c. celery leaves, chopped 
- 1 can sliced mushrooms
- 1/2 tsp salt
- dash pepper 
- 1/4 tsp tyme
- 4 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 c. cumbled bleu cheese crackers 
- 1 flank steak
- 1 c. water or stock

## Directions

1. Melt butter in skillet, saute onions and celery until tender but not browned. 
2. Add celery leaves, mushrooms, salt, and pepper, type, parsley, crackers, and stock. 
3. Pound steak to 1/4 in thick. Scare one side only. On unscared side, spread dressing. 
4. Roll and fasten with toothpicks or tie. Brown meat on all sides in fat in large skillet. 
5. Place meat on a rack in covered pan and bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours to 2 hours, until tender. 
6. Slice and serve with gravy from drippings.
