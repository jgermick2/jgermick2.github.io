## Ingredients

- 4 lb. boneless Rump Roast (Beef or Venison)
- 1 can beef broth
- 1 pkg. Onion Soup mix
- 1/2 jar peppercini (1/2 of juice also) small jar Del Alpe hot gardinera (if you like it spicy)
- 2 1/2 dozen small Gonnella rolls sliced raw onion

## Directions

1. Put first five ingredients in a crock pot and cook @ medium for 7 hours. 
2. Serve with peppercini and sliced raw onions.
