## Background

This is Mom's Recipe

## Ingredients

- 1 package of sauerkraut polish sausage (fresh and/or smoked)
- Instant potatoes
- Caraway seeds 
- Paprika
- Can of diced tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cook sausage. Remove sausage, cool & cut in serving pieces. Set aside. 
2. Put sauerkraut in greasy water & simmer. Than add some instant mashed potatoes to thicken. Add a can of diced tomatoes. Add caraway seeds. Then add sausage. Add paprika to color. 
3. Bring to boil. Simmer 45 minutes or so.
