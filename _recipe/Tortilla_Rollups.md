## Ingredients

- 16 oz cream cheese 
- 2 cans green chilies
- 3 green onions, chopped tabasco sauce
- 1 pkg large flour tortilla shells

## Directions

1. Mix first 4 ingredients. 
2. Spread on tortillas. 
3. Roll up and refrigerate. Cut in rounds.
