## Ingredients

- 4 (1oz) squares unsweetened chocolate
- ¾ cup butter 
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract 
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Microwave chocolate and butter in a large bowl at high for 2 minutes until butter is melted.
3. Stir until chocolate is melted. Stir in sugar. Mix in eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour and nuts.
4. Spread in greased 13 X 9 inch pan. Bake for 35 minutes (do not over bake).
