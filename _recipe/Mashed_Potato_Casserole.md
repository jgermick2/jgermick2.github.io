## Ingredients

- 3 lb potatoes, cooked & drained 
- 1/2 stick Butter
- 12 oz. Sour Cream
- 1 1/4 tsp. Pepper 
- 1 1/2 tsp. Salt 
- Bread Crumbs

## Directions

1. Mash first 5 ingredients together. 
2. Top with buttered bread crumbs. 
3. Refrigerate till needed. 
4. Bake @ 350 for 1 hour.
