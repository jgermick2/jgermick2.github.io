## Ingredients

- 8oz sour cream
- ¼ c lemon juice 
- 2-3 Tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp dill weed
- 1 cucumber, seeded and sliced 
- 1 sweet white onion, sliced

## Directions

1. Combine sour cream, lemon juice, sugar, salt and dill weed. Mix well. Add cucumber and onion. 
2. Cover and chill. 

Makes 3 cups.
