## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening 
- 1 1/2 cups sugar 
- 2 eggs
- 2 3/4 cups sifted flour
- 2 tsp cream of tartar 
- 1 tsp baking soda 
- 1/4 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400 deg. 
2. Mix shortening, sugar and eggs thoroughly. Blend flour, cream of tartar, soda and salt. Stir into shortening mixture. 
3. Shape dough into 1" balls. Roll in mixture of 2 tbsp sugar and cinnamon. 
4. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet and bake 8-10 minutes.
