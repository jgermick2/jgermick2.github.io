## Ingredients

- 6 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips 
- 1 tbsp shortening
- 1 pint strawberries 
- 1 c. heavy cream
- 1/3 c. confectioners sugar

## Directions

1. Place fluted baking cup in each of eight 3 in cups of muffin pan.
2. Melt chocolate together with shortening. Lift paper cup, tilt slightly, drizzle side to cover sides and bottom evenly.
3. Refrigerate until chocolate is firm.
4. With cooled hands, peel paper from each cup, leaving a chocolate cup. Set on chilled dessert platter and refrigerate. 
5. Cut berries into 1/8 in thick slices, reserving 1/2 c. for garnish. 
6. Beat whipping cream and sugar to form stiff peaks. Fold in strawberries. Spoon into cups. 
7. Garnish with remaining strawberries.
