## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup chopped walnuts 
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 cup chopped dates
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ cup cutter
- ½ tsp baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar. Add 2 eggs, beat well. Add flour, baking powder, milk and vanilla. 
2. Beat well and then add dates and walnuts. Pour into greased loaf pan. 
3. Bake 40 minutes at 350 degrees. Best served warm with whipped cream.
