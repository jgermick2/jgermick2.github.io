## Ingredients

- 4 cooked chicken breasts, cubed 
- 1 head lettuce, chopped
- 1/2 head red cabbage, chopped 
- 1/2 c. onion, chopped
- 1/4 c. poppy seeds
- 1/4 c. slivered almonds
- 1 c. La Choy rice noodles

Dressing:
- 4 tbs. sugar
- 4 tbs. vinigar 
- 1/2 c. oil
- 1 tbsp accent
- 1 tsp salt

## Directions

1. Toss all salad ingredients except rice noodles. 
2. Mix dressing ingredients and shake thoroughly.
3. Pour dressing over salad. 
4. Toss. Top with rice noodles. Serve immediately
