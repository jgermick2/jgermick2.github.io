## Ingredients

- 2 lb. Ground Chuck, browned & drained
- 3 cups green pepper, chopped 
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 29 oz. can tomato sauce
- 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes (can be with green chilies)
- 2 - 3 cups cooked rice
- 1/4 cup packed brown sugar 
- 1 tsp. pepper
- 2 tsp. salt
- 2 cubes beef bouillon

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together in a large kettle. 
2. Fill both empty cans with water and add to kettle. 
3. Simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
