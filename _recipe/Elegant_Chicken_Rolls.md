## Ingredients

- 6 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (4-6 oz each)
- 4 oz. cream cheese
- 4 oz. crumbled feta cheese
- 2 T. fresh basil leaves, snipped 
- 1/2 tsp coarsely ground black pepper
- 6 T. sweet roasted red peppers, drained and chopped
- 1 1/4 cups seasoned croutons, grated (1 cup crumbs)
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp milk
- 12-18 large spinach leaves
- 6 oz. thinly sliced deli smoked ham

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375 deg F. Butterfly cut each chicken breast half by inserting tip of knife into thickest side of chicken then cutting lengthwise through middle of breast, almost through to the other side. Spread chicken breast open flat. Place each chicken breast between 2 pieces of parchment paper.
Flatten chicken to at least 1/4 inch thickness. Set aside.

2. Combine cream cheese, feta cheese, basil snipped with kitchen shears and black pepper; blend well and set aside. Chop red peppers and drain on paper towels.

3. Grate croutons into shallow bowl. Whisk egg and milk together.

4. For each chicken roll, cover chicken breast with single layer of spinach leaves, top with scoop of cream cheese mixture, spread to within 1/4 inch of edge.

5. Sprinkle evenly with 1 tbsp of the red peppers and cover with 2 slices of ham. Roll up, jelly-roll fashion, tucking in ends when possible.

6. Dip rolls in egg mixture; coat with crumbs. Place seam side down in square, sprayed baking pan. Bake 35 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
