## Ingredients

1 pkg strawberry cake mix
4 oz frozen whipped topping 
2 large eggs, beaten
3/4 cup confectioners' sugar

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 deg. Grease baking sheets.

2. Combine cake mix, whipped topping and eggs in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer set at medium speed just until mixed; do not overbeat.

3. Shape dough into balls using heaping teaspoonfuls. Drop into confectioners' sugar, turning with spoons to coat. Arrange on prepared baking sheets.

4. Bake just until set, about 8 minutes.
