## Ingredients

- 3 lbs tart cooking apples 
- 1 c water
- 1 ¼ c sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp cornstarch
- ¼ c cold water

Crust:
- ¾ c lard 
- 2 c flour
- ½ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks, beaten
- ½ c water

## Directions

1. Pare, core and cut apples into eighths. Bring water, sugar, cinnamon and salt to boiling. Add apples and cook slowly for 10 minutes. 
2. Blend cornstarch and ¼ cup cold water; add to hot mixture. Cook 5 minutes longer, stirring gently. 
3. To make crust: Cut lard into sifted flour, baking powder and salt, as for pie crust. 
4. Mix lemon juice, egg yolks and water together and sprinkle over flour mixture. Blend it in lightly. Divide into two parts. Roll first piece to fit bottom and sides of a shallow pan, about 9 X13 inches.
5. Fill with apple mixture. Roll remaining dough to fit top; seal edges. Cut a design for steam vents.
6. Bake at 450 degrees for 20 minutes; reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake 30 minutes longer.
7. Ice with thin confectioners’ sugar frosting or sprinkle with confectioners’ sugar. Cut into squares to serve.
