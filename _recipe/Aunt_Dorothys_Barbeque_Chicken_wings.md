## Ingredients

4 lbs frozen wings small onion
1/2 veg. oil
1/2 red wine vinegar
1 tbs worcestershire sauce
1/2 c. lemon juice
1/4 c. firmly packed brown sugar 2 c. ketchup
    1 tbs paprika salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Saute onion in oil. Add all other ingredients except wings.
2. Simmer 20 min. Cool. Pour over wings and refrigerate 24 hours.
3. Bake at 350 for 45 min. or grill.
