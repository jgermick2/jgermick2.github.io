## Ingredients
Variations:
-> Jalapeno Baked Brie: Substitute chopped green pepper, pecans, and jalapeno jelly.
-> Apricot-Almond: Substitute diced dried apricots, almonds, apricot preserves. Add 1 tsp Dijon mustard.

- 1/4 c. dried cranberries 
- 1/4 c. walnuts
- 1 tbsp snipped parsley 
- 2 tbsp currant jelly
- 1 pkg crescent rolls
- 1 4 in round brie or Camembert cheese, rind included
- 1 egg, lightly beaten 
- apple or pear wedges


## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350. 
2. Combine cranberries, walnuts,parley, and jelly. 
3. Unroll crescent dough. Separate dough crosswise into two squares . 
4. Place Brie on one square of dough. Trim corners from first dough square, leaving 1-in border around brie. 
5. Scoop mixture over top. Cut a a couple shapes from corner and center of remaining square. Place dough over brie, pinching seams to seal. 
6. Place cut outs on top. Brush with egg. 
7. Bake 30-35 min. Let stand 15 min. before serving. 
8. Serve with apple and pear wedges.
