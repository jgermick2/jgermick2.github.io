## Ingredients

- 2/ 16 oz bags frozen, roasted corn

Sauce:
- 1 c. Mayo
- 1 Tbsp Chalula hot sauce 
- 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
- 1 tsp each: kosher salt, black pepper, sugar 
- 1/4 c. chicken stock
- 1/4 c. crumbled Cotija cheese some flour to thicken

Garnish:
- 1/4 c. crumbled Cotija Cheese 
- Ground Red Chili Pepper
- 2 tsp. Cilantro

## Directions

1. Cook corn and set aside. 
2. Mix all sauce ingredients together over medium heat. 
3. Thicken with flour to desired consistency. 
4. Stir in the cooked corn and garnish with cilantro, additional cotija cheese and ground Chili Pepper.

Serve with tortilla chips. Crazy good!

FYI - Frozen roasted corn is sometimes hard to find in my area so I have used regular frozen whole kernel corn and it turned out equally delicious. The original recipe called for you to char 6 ears of corn on a medium high grill and then shuck off the corn first. I wasn't about to but you feel free to go for it.
