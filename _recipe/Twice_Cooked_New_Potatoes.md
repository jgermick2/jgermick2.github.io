## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds new red potatoes, scrubbed and sliced thick
- 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
- 1/2 tsp salt 
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/3 cup Italian salad dressing 
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives

## Directions

1. Combine potatoes, broth and salt in a large skillet. 
2. Cook over medium-high heat until potatoes are tender, about 10 minutes; 
3. drain the skillet. Add butter to potatoes in skillet. Drizzle with salad dressing. 
4. Cook potato mixture over medium heat for 7 minutes. 
5. Sprinkle with chives. Serve immediately.
