## Ingredients

- 1 whole chicken, stewed & deboned 
- 2 cans cream of chicken soup
- 1 pkg. Pepperidge Farms Herbal Stuffing
- 1 stick butter
- 8 oz. sour cream

## Directions

1. Stew chicken in 2 qt. water till tender (approximately 1 hour). Drain & reserve 2 cups chicken broth. De-bone & shred chicken into small pieces and place in bottom of 9" X 13" casserole dish.

2. In a bowl, mix soup & sour cream. Pour mixture over the chicken. Pour package of stuffing evenly over the soup layer. Melt butter and pour over the stuffing mix. Pour 2 cups of reserved chicken broth over all.

3. Bake @ 350 for 45 minutes, making sure the top of the casserole is brown.
