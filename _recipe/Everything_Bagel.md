## Ingredients

- 8 oz. cream cheese 
- 8 oz. sour cream
- 3 tsp. Everything Bagel spice (from Aldi's or Trader Joe's)

## Directions

Mix together and serve with Pretzel crackers or raw veggies. YUM!
