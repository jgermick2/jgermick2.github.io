## Ingredients

- 4 chicken breasts, frozen
- 1 pkg Good Seasons Italian Dressing Mix
- 8 oz can sliced mushrooms
- 1 can cream of chicken soup 
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1 lb Egg Noodles or Penne Pasta

## Directions

1. Cook Chicken and Dressing Mix in crock pot 8 hrs on low or 5 hrs on high. Then shred chicken in the crock pot. 
2. Add cream cheese, cans of soup and mushrooms (WITH its liquid), and noodles. 
3. Serve when the cream cheese has melted.
