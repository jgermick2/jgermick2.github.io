## Ingredients

- 2 pkg. crescent rolls
- 2 - 8 oz pkg cream cheese 
- 1 c. mayonnaise
- 1 1/2 tsp. dill weed
- 1 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 c. each of cauliflower, broccoli, green onion, green pepper, carrot, mushrooms, green olives, black olives
- 8 oz mozzarella cheese

## Directions

1. Grease 11x15 pan. 
2. Unroll rolls and press into pan. Pinch dough to make crust. 3. Cook until done (15 min.) at 375. Let cool. 
4. Cream together cream cheese, mayonnaise, and seasonings. Spread on cooled crust. 
5. Top with vegetables and mozzarella cheese. Chill, covered, until ready to serve.
