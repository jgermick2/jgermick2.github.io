## Background

This is Mom's recipe

## Ingredients

1/3 cup Bisquick
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese 
1/8 tsp pepper
2 slightly beaten eggs
2 cups shredded zucchini

## Directions

1. Mix Bisquick, cheese, pepper. Add eggs & fold in zucchini. 
2. Fry in butter or bacon fat.
