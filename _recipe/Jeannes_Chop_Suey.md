## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs chop suey meat or pork and veal
- 3 c. water
- 1/4 c. chopped onion 
- 1/2 c. celery
- 1 can chop suey vegetables 
- 1 tbs. bead molasses
- 2 tbs. corn starch 
- 3 tbs. soy sauce

## Directions

1. Boil celery in water.
2. Saute onion. Brown meat.
3. Combine celery along with water and sauted onion with browned meat Add: Molasses, soy sauce, salt and pepper and simmer 1 hour. 
4. Add chop suey veggetables. 
5. Mix cornstarch with 1/4 c. water. 
6. Stir into Chop suey andsimmer until thickened, stirring occasionally. 
7. Serve over rice or chop suey noodles.
