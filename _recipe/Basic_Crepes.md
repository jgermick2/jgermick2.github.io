## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 eggs, slightly beaten 
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/3 cup water
- 3/4 cup flour 
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp baking powder

## Directions

1. Melt butter in omelet pan or crepe pan. Beat together eggs, milk and water with electric mixer or rotary beater, beating in melted butter, too. 
2. Blend in flour, salt and baking powder until mixture is smooth.
3. Refrigerate batter at least one hour. 
4. Use 1/4 cup batter for each crepe. 
5. Stack between sheets of wax paper.
