## Ingredients

- Chicken breasts 
- celery-chopped
- green onions-chopped
- mayonnaise 
- sliced almonds

## Directions

1. Poach chicken breasts. (Bring enough water to cover chicken to boil. Add chicken. Return to boil. Cover and turn off heat. Chicken will be cooked in 1 hour).
2. Cool and cut up chicken. ( I used to use a whole chicken, but people liked it better when I only used chicken breasts.)
3. Add celery, green onions, mayonnaise and sliced almonds.

I have seen grapes cut in half added to the chicken salad. I never tried it.
