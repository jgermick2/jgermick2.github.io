## Ingredients

- 1 pkg yeast
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 1/3 cup water
- 1/3 cup brown sugar 5 cup flour
- baking soda kosher salt

## Directions

1. Mix yeast and water - let set a few minutes. 
2. Add rest of water, brown sugar and as much flour as necessary up to 5 cups. Roll pretzels into shapes.

3. Boil baking soda in water, drop in pretzel for 30 sec each. 
4. Place in jelly roll pan, sprinkle with kosher salt. 
5. Bake at 475 degrees for 8 minutes.
