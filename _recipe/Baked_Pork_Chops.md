## Ingredients

- 1 can onion soup 
- 1 can celery soup 
- 1/2 c. water
- 1 tsp poultry seasoning 
- 1/8 tsp sage
- 1/4 tsp pepper 
- 1 tsp paprika
- 4 lean pork chops 
- 1 med onion, sliced

## Directions

1. Mix soups, water and seaonings. 
2. Pour into foil lined baking pan or casserole. 
3. Place chops in mixture. 
4. Top each with onion slice. 
5. Bake at 350 for 1 hour or til tender.
