## Ingredients

1 can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk 
1/2 cup Key Lime Juice (Nellie & Joe's)
8 oz. Cool Whip
Graham Cracker Pie Crust

## Directions

Blend all together & dump into pie crust. Refrigerate.
