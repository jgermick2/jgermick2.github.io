## Ingredients

- 7 oz. cheese tortellini, cooked & cooled
- 1 cup broccoli florets, lightly steamed
- 1/4 cup (or less) parsley
- 6 oz. marinated artichoke hearts, undrained
- 2 green onions, chopped 
- 1/4 tsp. basil
- 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
- 1/2 cup Zesty Italian dressing 
- 5-6 cherry tomatoes, halved sliced black olives
- Parmesan Cheese

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients except for tomatoes, olives & cheese. Add those just before serving.
