## Ingredients

- 2 c. dry elbow macaroni, cooked & set aside
- 4 Tbl salted butter, cubed
- 3 c. cheddar cheese, shredded 
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 1/2 c. light sour cream
- 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed Cheddar Cheese soup
- 1/2 c. heavy cream 
- 1/2 c. milk
- 1/2 tsp dry mustard salt & pepper, to taste

## Directions

1. Melt butter & shredded cheese in a sauce pan.
2. Combine melted cheese, eggs, sour cream, soup, cream, milk, mustard, and salt & pepper in a slow cooker. 
3. Stir in the macaroni and mix well together. 
4. Cook on low for 1 1/2 hrs, stirring occasionally. Serve @ enjoy!
