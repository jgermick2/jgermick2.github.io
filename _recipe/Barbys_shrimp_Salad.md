## Ingredients

- 1 lb Elbow Macaroni
- 2 cans Med. Shrimp, drained 
- Miracle Whip - lots
- chopped onion 
- chopped celery
- 1 can peas, drained 
- Lawrey's Seasoning - lots 
- Pepper - lots

## Directions

Mix all together. BTW - doubling this recipe equals a "Memorial Day" sized salad.
