## Ingredients

- 4 lbs baby back pork ribs 
- 4 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1 tbsp white sugar 
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp pepper
- 2 tsp chili powder 
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 c. dark brown sugar
- 1/2 c. cider vinegar 
- 1/2 c. ketchup
- 1/4 c. chili sauce
- 1/4 c. Worcestershire 
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp onion, chopped 
- 1/2 tsp dry mustard
- 1 clove crushed garlic

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees. 
2. Place ribs on a rack in a shallow roasting pan. 
3. Scatter garlic slices over ribs. Cover, and bake for 2 1/2 hours. Cool slightly. 
4. In a small brown, mix white sugar, paprika, salt, pepper, chili powder, and cumin. Rub spices over cooled ribs. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
5. In small saucepan, mix remaining ingredients. Simmer over medium low heat, uncovered, for 1 hour. 
6. Reserve a small amount of sauce for dipping. Grill, covered, for about 12 min, basting with the reserved sauce, until nicely browned and glazed.
