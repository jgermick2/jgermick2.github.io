## Ingredients

-1-stick butter
-1/4 cup of olive oil
-1/4 cup green onions, chopped 
-1 tbsp. garlic, minced
-2 lbs chicken breasts, boneless, cut into 1/2" pieces
-Juice of one lemon 
-1 tsp. salt
-1/2 tsp. black pepper
-1/4 cup fresh parsley, minced 
-1 tomato, chopped
-Cooked hot rice

## Directions

1. In skillet, heat together butter and olive oil and saute onions and garlic. 
2. Add chicken, lemon juice, salt, pepper and parsley. 
3. Continue cooking, stirring constantly, for 5-8 minutes or until chicken is done. Add tomatoes and heat through. 
4. Serve over hot rice.
