## Background

My recipe for these says it is from "Family Circle 1972."

## Ingredients

- 1-3oz or 4oz pkg cream cheese, softened
- 1 stick of butter, softened 
- 1 c. sifted all purpose flour 
- 1 egg, slightly beaten
- 3/4 c. firmly packed brown sugar 
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tbs softened butter 
- dash of salt
- 2/3 c. chopped pecans

## Directions

1. Blend cream cheese and 1 stick of butter until smooth. Add flour. Blend well. Chill for 2 hours or overnight.
2. Shape into 24 - 1 inch balls. Press into mini muffin or tart pans. Place pans on Jelly roll pan.
3. Blend eggs, brown sugar, vanilla, 1 tbs butter, salt, and pecans. Fill cups with mixture.
4. Bake at 375 degrees for 20 min or until lightly browned. Cool for a minute or two on wire racks before removing from pan.
